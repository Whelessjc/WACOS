# AI Workflows

## Purpose
Document safe and useful AI workflows for culinary instruction, Bistro operations, assessment, tutoring, administration, and documentation maintenance.

## AI Principles
- AI may draft, organize, compare, and generate options.
- AI must not invent program facts, grades, safety rules, allergens, or district policy.
- Instructor review is required before using AI-generated assessments, recipes, safety instructions, or family/admin communications.
- Student-facing AI should coach thinking rather than simply provide answers.
- Source-bound work is preferred for repository updates.

## Approved Workflows
| Workflow | Use |
|---|---|
| Lesson planning | Draft daily plans from WACOS, calendar constraints, and skill targets |
| Bistro prep support | Convert menu into prep list, station plan, and timeline for instructor review |
| Recipe scaling tutor | Coach students through scaling and conversions |
| Skill checklist tutor | Ask scenario questions and line checks |
| Reflection analysis | Summarize service reflections and identify reteach needs |
| Assessment drafting | Draft rubrics or practical checks for instructor approval |
| Knowledge base maintenance | Compare new notes against WACOS and propose updates |

## Guardrails
- Do not give students unreviewed food safety instructions.
- Do not let AI replace instructor scoring of live performance.
- Do not upload private personal transcripts into a broad student-facing GPT.
- Require citations or source references when AI proposes policy or program facts.
