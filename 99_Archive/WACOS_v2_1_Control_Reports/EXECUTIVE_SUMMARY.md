# Executive Summary

## Summary
WACOS Version 2.1 is the cleaned operating release of the West Ashley Culinary Operating System. It keeps the source-backed Version 2.0 reconstruction while improving organization, terminology, decision tracking, and repository hygiene.

## Program Model
- Culinary 1 is a one-semester foundation and exploration course.
- Culinary 2 is a full-year depth and operations course built around fundamentals under pressure.
- The Bistro is the student-led restaurant/service experience and the major applied-learning environment.
- Hospitality Work-Based Learning is the next application stage after or alongside advanced pathway work.

## QA Improvements
- Evidence moved to the archive.
- Exports moved out of the operating repo.
- Decision conflicts centralized.
- Terminology standardized.
- Internal links checked.

## Review Needed
The largest unresolved items are grade weights, Bistro launch timing, credential sequence, recipe deduplication, and facility-specific SOPs.
