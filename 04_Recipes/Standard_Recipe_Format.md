# Standard Recipe Format

Source: `09_Recipes_and_Production.md` (West Ashley Culinary operating documentation). This is the format every entry in the Recipe Bank follows - use it exactly when processing the historical archive.

## Key Principle

Recipes are vehicles for skill practice. The curriculum is the skill, habit, and judgment being developed through the recipe.

## Every Recipe Entry Should Include

- Name.
- Course/use case: Culinary 1, Culinary 2, Bistro, catering, assessment.
- Source and revision date.
- Yield and portion size.
- Equipment.
- Ingredients with units.
- Procedure.
- Food safety controls.
- Allergens.
- Quality indicators.
- Holding, cooling, storage, reheating.
- Scaling notes.
- Skill focus.
