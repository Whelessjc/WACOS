# 04_Recipes

This folder contains recipe guidance and approved recipe-bank materials.

## Belongs Here

- Recipe standards.
- Recipe use guidelines.
- Production recipe guidance.
- Approved recipe bank documents.
- Recipe scaling and yield guidance.

## Does Not Belong Here

- Unreviewed generated recipe dumps.
- Raw archive recipe conversations.
- Bistro prep lists unless they are recipe packets.
- General curriculum documents.

## Relationship to the Core Manual

The Core Manual states that recipes are vehicles for skill practice, not the curriculum itself.

## Caution

The master recipe bank still needs review, deduplication, source dating, and instructor approval before being treated as fully authoritative.
