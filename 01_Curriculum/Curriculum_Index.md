## Program Progression

Culinary 1 establishes the habits that make the kitchen work: uniform readiness, sanitation, recipe reading, mise en place, knife control, heat control, teamwork, beginning service language, and responsible cleanup.

Culinary 2 raises the standard. Students repeat the fundamentals with greater independence, connect skills to Bistro production, demonstrate composure under pressure, and gather evidence toward practical kitchen readiness.

The intended progression is not novelty. The intended progression is responsibility.

## Storage Rule

Do not operate Culinary 1 from Desktop and Culinary 2 from OneDrive.

Use this repository as the organized curriculum home. External folders may remain useful as working copies, backups, or source imports, but current curriculum decisions should be made in WACOS and pushed to GitHub.
