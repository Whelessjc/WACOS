# Frying and Oil Management Lab Student Handout
## Purpose
This lab helps you practice Unit 08 skills in a controlled, useful kitchen task.
## Skills Practiced
- oil safety
- fryer safety
- temperature awareness
- batch control
- overcrowding
- crisp vs. greasy
- draining
- seasoning timing
- potato batonnet
- vegetable fritters
- onion rings or similar low-cost applications
## Equipment / Materials
Your teacher will assign the tools, equipment, and products for the day. Check the board, huddle notes, and station directions before starting.
## Possible Applications
- teacher-led oil safety observation
- potato batonnet and fries practice
- vegetable fritters
- onion rings or similar low-cost frying task
- oil-station reset
## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Task read before acting
- [ ] Tools gathered
- [ ] Ingredients or materials checked
- [ ] Station safety checked
- [ ] Dish/reset plan clear
## Procedure
. Complete entry routine and handwashing.
. Listen to the demo or mini-lesson.
. Set up the station before starting.
. Practice the assigned skill at a controlled pace.
. Pause for teacher checkpoint.
. Complete the lab or service application.
. Clean, store, dish, and reset fully.
. Complete reflection or debrief.
## Teamwork Expectations
- Stay in your role unless reassigned.
- Use clear, useful communication.
- Ask for help before the station falls behind.
- Share dish, towels, trash, floor, and reset.
## Safety Reminders
- Keep water away from hot oil.
- Limit students at the oil station.
- Dry food before frying as directed.
- Use tools, not fingers, near oil.
- Stop immediately for spills, crowding, or unsafe movement.
## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Oil handled only as directed
- [ ] Tools cleaned and stored
- [ ] Draining area cleaned
- [ ] Counters degreased and sanitized as directed
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash and floors checked carefully
- [ ] Station ready for the next group
## Reflection Questions
1. What skill did you practice?
2. What did you notice while working?
3. What correction helped?
4. How could this support Bistro service?
5. What reset job did you complete?

## Frying Checkpoints
- Potato batonnet/fries: cut evenly, dry well, fry in controlled batches, drain, then season.
- Vegetable fritters: control batter amount, batch size, draining, and greasy surfaces.
- Onion rings or similar item: load and remove safely with the correct tool.
