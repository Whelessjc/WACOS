# Canvas Module: Unit 08 Frying and Oil Management
## Module Title
Unit 08: Frying and Oil Management
## Overview Text
Week 13 focus: Temperature discipline.
Students practice real kitchen habits through safety, setup, lab work, communication, stewardship, and reset. This module connects the unit skills to Bistro service when useful.
## Suggested Module Order
1. Page: Unit-08-Student-Packet.md
2. Page: Frying-and-Oil-Safety-Student-Handout.md
3. Assignment: Unit 08 Skill Check
4. Lab Activity: Frying-Lab-Student-Handout.md
5. Checklist: Unit-08-Printable-Checklist.md
6. Discussion/Reflection: Unit 08 Reflection
## Pages
### Page: Unit Overview
Include:

- Why it matters in the kitchen
- What success looks like
- Safety expectations
- Cleanup/reset expectations

Use Frying-and-Oil-Safety-Student-Handout.md.

## Assignments
### Assignment: Unit 08 Skill Check
Students answer:
- What skill are we practicing?
- What safety habit matters most?
- What should the station look like before work starts?
- How does this connect to cooking, Bistro, or readiness?
## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: What habit from this unit made the kitchen safer, cleaner, or more ready for service?
Students can answer in 3 to 5 sentences after the lab or service activity.
