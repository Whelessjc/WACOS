# Protein as Application Lab Student Handout
## Purpose
This lab helps you practice Unit 10 skills in a controlled, useful kitchen task.
## Skills Practiced
- chicken breast or thigh
- ground turkey
- egg cookery
- searing
- doneness
- resting
- seasoning
- slicing
- temperature checking
- food safety
- knife control, heat control, mise en place, and flavor balance
## Equipment / Materials
Your teacher will assign the tools, equipment, and products for the day. Check the board, huddle notes, and station directions before starting.
## Possible Applications
- egg cookery
- ground turkey application
- thermometer practice
- chicken breast or thigh lab
- searing and resting practice
- protein sanitation reset
## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Task read before acting
- [ ] Tools gathered
- [ ] Ingredients or materials checked
- [ ] Station safety checked
- [ ] Dish/reset plan clear
## Procedure
. Complete entry routine and handwashing.
. Listen to the demo or mini-lesson.
. Set up the station before starting.
. Practice the assigned skill at a controlled pace.
. Pause for teacher checkpoint.
. Complete the lab or service application.
. Clean, store, dish, and reset fully.
. Complete reflection or debrief.
## Teamwork Expectations
- Stay in your role unless reassigned.
- Use clear, useful communication.
- Ask for help before the station falls behind.
- Share dish, towels, trash, floor, and reset.
## Safety Reminders
- Wash hands after raw protein contact.
- Keep raw and cooked tools separate.
- Clean thermometer probes before and after use.
- Verify doneness when required.
- Sanitize raw-contact surfaces completely.
## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Raw-contact surfaces sanitized
- [ ] Thermometers cleaned and returned
- [ ] Raw and clean tools washed separately as directed
- [ ] Boards and knives handled safely
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Towels, trash, and floors checked
- [ ] Station safe for the next group
## Reflection Questions
1. What skill did you practice?
2. What did you notice while working?
3. What correction helped?
4. How could this support Bistro service?
5. What reset job did you complete?

## Protein Lab Checkpoints
- Eggs: use gentle heat and clean egg-contact surfaces.
- Ground turkey: prevent cross contamination and verify doneness as directed.
- Chicken breast or thigh: control raw station, sear safely, check temperature, rest, slice, and reset.
