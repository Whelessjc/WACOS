# Canvas Module: Unit 02 Food Handler and Stewardship
## Module Title
Unit 02: Food Handler and Stewardship
## Overview Text
Week 2 focus: Safety becomes behavior.
Students practice real kitchen habits through safety, setup, lab work, communication, stewardship, and reset. This module connects the unit skills to Bistro service when useful.
## Suggested Module Order
1. Page: Unit-02-Student-Packet.md
2. Page: Stewardship-Jobs-Student-Handout.md
3. Assignment: Unit 02 Skill Check
4. Lab Activity: Food-Handler-Lab-Student-Handout.md
5. Checklist: Unit-02-Printable-Checklist.md
6. Discussion/Reflection: Unit 02 Reflection
## Pages
### Page: Unit Overview
Include:
- What students are learning
- Why it matters in the kitchen
- What success looks like
- Safety expectations
- Cleanup/reset expectations
### Page: Student Handout
Use Stewardship-Jobs-Student-Handout.md.
Include:
- food safety
- personal hygiene
- cleaning and sanitation
- cross contamination
- allergens
- time and temperature control
## Assignments
### Assignment: Unit 02 Skill Check
Students answer:
- What skill are we practicing?
- What safety habit matters most?
- What should the station look like before work starts?
- How does this connect to cooking, Bistro, or readiness?
## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: What habit from this unit made the kitchen safer, cleaner, or more ready for service?
Students can answer in 3 to 5 sentences after the lab or service activity.
## Lab or Service Activity
Use Food-Handler-Lab-Student-Handout.md.

- Food-Handler-Lab-Teacher-Guide.md
## Checklist
Use Unit-02-Printable-Checklist.md as the student-facing checklist for setup, skill practice, sanitation, stewardship, reset, and exit routine.
## Suggested Completion Evidence
- Student follows safety expectations.
- Student completes the assigned practice or lab task.
- Student communicates with the team.
- Student supports cleanup and reset.
- Student completes reflection or debrief.
