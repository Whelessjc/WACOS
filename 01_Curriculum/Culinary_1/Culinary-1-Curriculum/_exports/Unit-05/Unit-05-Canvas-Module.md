# Canvas Module: Unit 05 Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Module Title
Unit 05: Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Overview Text
This week students strengthen Bistro systems before moving into deeper technical skills. They use the soft opening as evidence, retrain stations, clarify roles, practice ticket flow, run mock service 2.0, complete Bistro prep, and run a controlled Bistro service.

Service systems are practiced habits. Clear roles, readable tickets, useful communication, steady timing, active dish flow, sanitation, and full reset make the kitchen calmer and safer.

## Assignments

### Assignment: Soft Opening Debrief
Students answer:
- What worked during the soft opening?
- Where did the system slow down?
- What should the class practice next?

Students should use facts, not blame.

### Assignment: Station Role Check
Students answer:
- What is my station?
- What do I do before service?
- What do I do during service?
- What do I reset after service?
- Who do I ask when unsure?

## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: What system improved this week, and what system still needs practice before our technical skills get harder?

Students can answer in 3 to 5 sentences or complete a short exit response after Bistro service.

Optional sentence starters:
- One system that improved was...
- Ticket flow worked better when...
- Reset matters because...
