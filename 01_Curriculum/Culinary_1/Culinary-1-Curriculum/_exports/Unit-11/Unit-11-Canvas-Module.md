# Canvas Module: Unit 11 Bridge to Culinary 2
## Module Title
Unit 11: Bridge to Culinary 2
## Overview Text
Week 18 focus: Independence and readiness.
Students practice real kitchen habits through safety, setup, lab work, communication, stewardship, and reset. This module connects the unit skills to Bistro service when useful.
## Suggested Module Order
1. Page: Unit-11-Student-Packet.md
2. Page: Culinary-1-Skill-Inventory.md
3. Assignment: Unit 11 Skill Check
4. Lab Activity: Culinary-2-Readiness-Reflection.md
5. Checklist: Unit-11-Printable-Checklist.md
6. Discussion/Reflection: Unit 11 Reflection
## Pages
### Page: Unit Overview
Include:
- What students are learning
- Why it matters in the kitchen
- What success looks like
- Safety expectations
- Cleanup/reset expectations
### Page: Student Handout
Use Culinary-1-Skill-Inventory.md.
Include:
- independent prep list creation
- limited service ownership
- self-assessment
- skill inventory
- reflection
- Culinary 2 interest/readiness conversation
## Assignments
### Assignment: Unit 11 Skill Check
Students answer:
- What skill are we practicing?
- What safety habit matters most?
- What should the station look like before work starts?
- How does this connect to cooking, Bistro, or readiness?
## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: What habit from this unit made the kitchen safer, cleaner, or more ready for service?
Students can answer in 3 to 5 sentences after the lab or service activity.
## Lab or Service Activity
Use Culinary-2-Readiness-Reflection.md.
Teacher should use:
- Bridge-to-Culinary-2-Teacher-Guide.md
## Checklist
Use Unit-11-Printable-Checklist.md as the student-facing checklist for setup, skill practice, sanitation, stewardship, reset, and exit routine.
## Suggested Completion Evidence
- Student follows safety expectations.
- Student completes the assigned practice or lab task.
- Student communicates with the team.
- Student supports cleanup and reset.
- Student completes reflection or debrief.
