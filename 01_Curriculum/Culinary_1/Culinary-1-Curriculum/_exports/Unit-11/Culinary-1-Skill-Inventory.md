# Bridge to Culinary 2 Student Handout
## Purpose
This handout gives you the key habits for Unit 11: Independence and readiness.
The goal is practical kitchen confidence. Work safely, stay organized, use clear words, and reset the station for the next group.
## Key Skills
- independent prep list creation
- limited service ownership
- self-assessment
- skill inventory
- reflection
- Culinary 2 interest/readiness conversation
- transition from functioning in the kitchen to greater independence
- stewardship
## What To Watch For
- Safety before speed.
- Setup before action.
- Small corrections before big problems.
- Clean station before, during, and after work.
- Team communication that helps the task move.
## Bistro Connection
Students show Bistro and Culinary 2 readiness by planning work, communicating, completing a role, supporting the team, and resetting the kitchen without being chased.
## Student Self-Check
- [ ] I understand today's task.
- [ ] My station is set up.
- [ ] I followed safety expectations.
- [ ] I communicated when needed.
- [ ] I supported dish flow and reset.
- [ ] My station is ready for the next group.

## Skill Inventory Evidence
For each skill, write evidence from a real lab, service day, cleanup/reset, or team task. Do not write that you are ready unless you can name where you showed the habit.
