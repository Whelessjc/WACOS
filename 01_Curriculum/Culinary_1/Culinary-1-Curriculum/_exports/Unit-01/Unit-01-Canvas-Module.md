# Canvas Module: Unit 01 Orientation and Systems

## Module Title
Unit 01: Orientation and Systems

## Overview Text
Welcome to Culinary Arts Management 1. This week we will learn the room before cooking. You will practice the daily entry routine, uniform expectations, kitchen communication, handwashing, basic safety, measuring, station setup, cleanup/reset, and our first lab using pancakes.

This course is built around real cooking and real skills. Before we cook bigger labs, we need shared systems that keep the kitchen safe, organized, and ready for the next group.

## Pages

### Page: Unit 01 Overview
Students learn the focus for the week: learn the room before cooking.

Include:
- What we are learning
- Why routines matter
- What success looks like
- Friday pancake lab overview

### Page: Daily Kitchen Routine
Include:
- Enter calmly
- Put belongings away
- Check the agenda
- Check uniform/readiness
- Join the huddle
- Wash hands when directed
- Begin only when released

### Page: Uniform and Handwashing Expectations
Include:
- School-specific uniform policy
- Hair restraint, shoes, jewelry, and loose items
- When to wash hands
- Why handwashing protects the kitchen

### Page: Kitchen Communication and Basic Safety
Include:
- Behind
- Hot
- Sharp
- Corner
- Heard
- I need help
- Walk, report spills, ask before equipment

### Page: Measuring and Station Setup
Include:
- Dry measuring
- Liquid measuring
- Measuring spoons
- Read directions first
- Gather tools and ingredients
- Towel, trash, and dish plan

## Assignments

### Assignment: Unit 01 Student Packet Review
Students read the Unit 01 Student Packet and answer a short reflection:
- What routine do you already understand?
- What routine do you need to practice?
- What is one question you have before the first lab?

### Assignment: Measuring Station Practice
Students rotate through measuring stations and complete the teacher-provided practice task.

## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: Why do we learn systems before we cook?

Students should answer in 3 to 5 sentences or complete a quick written exit response.

Optional sentence starters:
- A kitchen needs systems because...
- One system I think will help me is...
- One routine I need to practice is...
