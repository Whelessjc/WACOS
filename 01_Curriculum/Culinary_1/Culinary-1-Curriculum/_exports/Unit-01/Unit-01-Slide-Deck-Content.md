# Unit 01 Slide Deck Content: Orientation and Systems

Use this content to build a classroom slideshow. Keep slide text short and visual-friendly.

## Slide 1: Welcome to Culinary Arts Management 1
- Real cooking
- Real skills
- Safe shared work
- Learn by doing

Speaker notes: Set the tone. This is a working kitchen course, not a sit-and-watch course.

## Slide 2: Week 1 Focus
- Learn the room before cooking
- Systems before speed
- Cooking includes cleaning
- Ask before guessing

Speaker notes: Explain that the first week slows down so the rest of the semester can work.

## Slide 3: What This Course Builds
- Safety
- Sanitation
- Organization
- Teamwork
- Kitchen confidence

Speaker notes: Connect these habits to future labs and Bistro readiness.

## Slide 4: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Put belongings away
- Check the agenda
- Join the huddle
- Wait to begin

Speaker notes: Practice this routine repeatedly during Week 1.

## Slide 5: Uniform Readiness
- Follow school policy
- Hair restrained
- Safe shoes
- No loose items
- Ready to work

Speaker notes: Add school-specific uniform language out loud.

## Slide 6: Kitchen Communication
- Behind
- Hot
- Sharp
- Corner
- Heard

Speaker notes: Have students practice saying these calmly and clearly.

## Slide 7: Handwashing
- Before food work
- After contamination
- After restroom
- After coughing or sneezing
- When directed to reset

Speaker notes: Emphasize that handwashing is a kitchen behavior, not just a rule.

## Slide 8: Basic Safety
- Walk
- Keep pathways clear
- Report spills
- Ask before equipment
- Treat hot as hot

Speaker notes: Keep this practical. Point to actual areas of the room.

## Slide 9: Measuring Stations
- Choose the right tool
- Dry measuring
- Liquid measuring
- Measuring spoons
- Return tools

Speaker notes: Use real tools when possible.

## Slide 10: Mise en Place
- Read first
- Gather tools
- Gather ingredients
- Set the station
- Know your role

Speaker notes: Keep the definition simple: ready before starting.

## Slide 11: Cleanup and Reset
- Dishes
- Counters sanitized
- Tools returned
- Towels handled
- Trash checked
- Floor and heat checked

Speaker notes: Make clear that cleanup is part of cooking.

## Slide 12: Friday Pancake Lab
- Read directions
- Measure carefully
- Work as a team
- Watch heat
- Reset fully

Speaker notes: The lab is about systems more than perfect pancakes.

## Slide 13: Bistro Starts Here
- Know the room
- Communicate clearly
- Wash hands
- Reset for the next group

Speaker notes: Keep the Bistro connection light but clear.

## Slide 14: What Success Looks Like
- Safe movement
- Clean hands
- Useful communication
- Careful measuring
- Complete reset

Speaker notes: These are look-fors during the week.

## Slide 15: Exit Reflection
- One routine I know
- One tool I can find
- One habit I need to practice
- One way I helped my team

Speaker notes: Use this as a quick written reflection or huddle response.
