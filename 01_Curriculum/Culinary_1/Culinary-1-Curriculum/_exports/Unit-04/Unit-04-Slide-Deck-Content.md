# Unit 04 Slide Deck Content: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

Use this content to build a classroom slideshow. Keep slide text short and visual-friendly.

## Slide 1: Unit 04
- Customer Service
- Bistro Soft Opening
- Full flow of service
- Service is a team system

Speaker notes: Introduce service as a system students can practice.

## Slide 2: Why This Unit Matters
- Guests experience the whole room
- Tickets need a clear path
- Stations work together
- Reset protects the next group

Speaker notes: Service includes both visible and behind-the-scenes work.

## Slide 3: Life of an Order
- Guest arrives
- Greet and take order
- Ticket moves
- Prepare and expo
- Run, clear, reset, farewell

Speaker notes: Use one sample order and walk it through the room.

## Slide 4: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Check agenda
- Join huddle
- Wash hands when directed
- Wait for release

Speaker notes: Keep the Unit 01 routine alive.

## Slide 5: Hospitality
- Respectful greeting
- Calm voice
- Guest awareness
- Follow through
- Farewell matters

Speaker notes: Hospitality is behavior students can practice.

## Slide 6: Station Roles
- Setup job
- Service job
- Ticket/task flow
- Reset job
- Ask when unsure

Speaker notes: Each role should have before, during, and after-service expectations.

## Slide 7: Ticket Flow
- Read the ticket
- Send it correctly
- Call back
- Keep tickets visible
- Close or reset

Speaker notes: Practice simple call-backs.

## Slide 8: Expo
- Organizes orders
- Checks accuracy
- Keeps handoff calm
- Limits crowding

Speaker notes: Expo is a control point, not a crowding point.

## Slide 9: Food Running and Check Back
- Move safely
- Use guest-friendly language
- Confirm the right order
- Notice what guests need

Speaker notes: Keep this simple and school-appropriate.

## Slide 10: Clearing and Dish
- Clear respectfully
- Move dishes correctly
- Keep dish flow moving
- Reset is service

Speaker notes: Dish and clearing are part of hospitality.

## Slide 11: Mock Service
- One order path
- Real communication
- Stop and fix
- Reset after each round

Speaker notes: Stop and reteach when the system breaks.

## Slide 12: Stewardship During Service
- Notice what needs doing
- Protect guest spaces
- Protect kitchen spaces
- Share reset work

Speaker notes: Stewardship is shared responsibility.

## Slide 13: Soft Opening
- Small and controlled
- Roles confirmed
- Ticket flow visible
- Dish flow active
- Debrief after service

Speaker notes: Keep expectations controlled.

## Slide 14: Safety During Service
- Walk with purpose
- Say "hot" and "behind"
- Keep paths clear
- Report spills

Speaker notes: Movement becomes harder under service pressure.

## Slide 15: Cleanup and Reset
- Guest area
- Service area
- Dish area
- Tools and towels
- Trash and floors

Speaker notes: Service is not finished until reset is finished.

## Slide 16: Exit Reflection
- Where can service break down?
- What role did you understand?
- What system needs practice?

Speaker notes: Use as an exit ticket or quick service debrief.
