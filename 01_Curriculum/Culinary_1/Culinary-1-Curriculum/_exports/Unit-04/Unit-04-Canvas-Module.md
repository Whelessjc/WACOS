# Canvas Module: Unit 04 Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Module Title
Unit 04: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Overview Text
This week students learn the full flow of Bistro service, from greeting to reset. They practice Life of an Order, station roles, ticket flow, expo, food running, dish flow, mock service, Bistro prep, and a controlled soft opening.

Hospitality is practiced behavior. Service works when students communicate clearly, know where an order goes next, move safely, care for guest and kitchen spaces, and reset for the next group.

## Assignments

### Assignment: Life of an Order Practice
Students trace one sample order from guest arrival to farewell.

Students should identify:
- Who touches the order
- Where the ticket goes
- What communication is needed
- Where the order could break down
- What reset is required

### Assignment: Station Role Check
Students answer:
- What is my station?
- What do I do before service?
- What do I do during service?
- What do I reset after service?
- Who do I ask when unsure?

## Discussion / Reflection
Prompt: Where can service break down, and what can a team do before it becomes a bigger problem?

Students can answer in 3 to 5 sentences or complete a short exit response after mock service.

Optional sentence starters:
- Service can break down when...
- One communication habit that helps is...
- Reset matters because...

## Lab / Service Activity
### Mock Service
Purpose: practice the full service flow slowly enough to stop, correct, and reset.

Students should use:
- Life-of-an-Order-Student-Handout.md
- Unit-04-Printable-Checklist.md

Teacher focus:
- One order path at a time
- Visible tickets
- Clear call-backs
- Reset after each round

### Bistro Soft Opening
Purpose: run a small controlled service with real expectations.

Students should use:
- Bistro-Soft-Opening-Student-Handout.md
- Unit-04-Printable-Checklist.md

Teacher should use:
- Bistro-Soft-Opening-Teacher-Guide.md

Teacher focus:
- Confirm roles before service
- Watch ticket flow, prep, service, dish, and reset
- Debrief one success and one system to practice
