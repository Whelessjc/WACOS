# Assessment Notes: Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Debrief response
- Station role explanation
- Ticket flow practice
- Mock service observation
- Prep list check
- Reset inspection
- Mid-service clean-as-you-go observation

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by improving a service role, using communication standards, completing prep, maintaining station cleanliness, noticing guest/team needs, and helping reset during Bistro service.

## Observation Points
- Does the student use feedback?
- Does the student know the role without prompting?
- Does the student communicate ticket status?
- Does the student support service instead of watching?
- Does the student notice guest experience or teammate needs?
- Does the student reset completely?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Improvement | Uses debrief feedback. |
| Role Clarity | Performs station job. |
| Communication | Uses clear call-backs and status updates. |
| Hospitality | Notices guest and teammate needs. |
| Prep/Service | Completes assigned tasks. |
| Stewardship | Notices dish, towel, storage, floor, and reset needs. |
| Reset | Leaves station ready. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can contribute to a controlled service with clearer role ownership and better communication than Week 4.
