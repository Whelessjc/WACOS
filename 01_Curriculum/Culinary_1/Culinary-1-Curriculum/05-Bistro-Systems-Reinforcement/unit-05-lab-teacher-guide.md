# Unit 05 Lab Teacher Guide: Bistro Systems Practice

## Setup Before Class
- Bring notes from the soft opening.
- Choose one or two systems to retrain first.
- Post station roles and communication standards.
- Prepare mock tickets or order slips.
- Stage simple Bistro prep items and service tools.
- Confirm dish, towel, storage, floor, and final reset assignments.

## Demo Points
- Model debrief language using facts.
- Show the station setup/reset standard.
- Model ticket call-back and "heard."
- Show how a mid-service reset looks.
- Show how prep should be labeled or stored.

## Safety Watch-Outs
- Students rushing under service pressure.
- Crowding at expo or dish.
- Hot food paths.
- Wet floors and loose towels.
- Students relaxing sanitation during mock service.

## Skill Checkpoints
- Student can state role and improvement target.
- Station is set before practice starts.
- Ticket language is short and clear.
- Prep list items are completed and stored correctly.
- Dish and reset happen throughout.
- Final station would be useful to the next group.

## Common Student Mistakes
- Debriefing with blame.
- Forgetting station duties after service starts.
- Not calling back ticket information.
- Waiting for the teacher to fix every issue.
- Letting dish, towels, or floors pile up.

## Cleanup Timing
- Use one planned reset pause during mock service.
- Start dish before final minutes.
- Check storage and labels before counters are wiped.
- Inspect stations before debrief.
- Have students fix incomplete reset immediately.

## What to Look For While Circulating
- Are roles visible in student actions?
- Are tickets moving clearly?
- Are students correcting calmly?
- Is guest awareness present?
- Is cleanup shared?
- Is the service area calmer than Week 4?

## Bistro Connection
This lab prepares students to carry Bistro systems into more technical units. Knife skills and heat labs will work better when roles, tickets, prep, and reset are already familiar.

## Quick Debrief Prompts
- What improved from soft opening?
- What station needs another practice round?
- What phrase helped ticket flow?
- What reset job protected the next group?
