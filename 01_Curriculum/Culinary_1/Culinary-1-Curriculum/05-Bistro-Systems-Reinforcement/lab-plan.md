# Lab Plan: Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Lab Purpose
Use service practice to strengthen roles, ticket flow, communication, prep, and reset before moving deeper into technical skills.

## Skill Focus
- Debrief and improvement
- Station retraining
- Ticket communication
- Hospitality and guest awareness
- Bistro prep
- Service reset

## Ingredients or Materials
- Teacher-selected Bistro prep items
- Tickets or order slips
- Station role notes
- Prep list

## Equipment
- Service station tools
- Prep tools
- Timers
- Towels and sanitizer
- Dish area

## Setup
- Review soft opening notes.
- Confirm station assignments.
- Prepare tickets for mock service 2.0.
- Post or review prep list.

## Procedure
1. Debrief soft opening.
2. Retrain station expectations.
3. Practice ticket flow.
4. Run mock service 2.0.
5. Complete Bistro prep with clean storage and labeling.
6. Run controlled Bistro service with guest awareness and clean-as-you-go.
7. Reset and debrief improvement.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- Students can state their station role.
- Ticket language is clear.
- Prep list items are completed.
- Dish and reset happen throughout service.
- Student improvement target is visible.
- Station would be usable by the next group.

## Cleanup and Reset Expectations
Every station returns to ready condition: tools stored, counters sanitized, dishes handled, towels managed, floors checked, service area reset.

## Possible Variations
- Run mock service without guests if needed.
- Limit service to one role rotation.
- Use a short station retraining circuit.
