# Lesson Sequence: Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Day 1: Soft Opening Debrief
- Opening routine: huddle with service reflection and guest-awareness question.
- Demo or mini-lesson: debrief with facts, not blame.
- Student work: name one strength, one guest-impact issue, and one station problem.
- Lab/application: station reset rehearsal using the soft opening problems.
- Cleanup/reset: reset practice with dish, towel, counter, and floor check.
- Exit check: one system to improve before the next service.

## Day 2: Station Retraining
- Opening routine: agenda check and role focus.
- Demo or mini-lesson: station expectations before, during, and after service, including what the guest never sees but still depends on.
- Student work: revise station responsibilities and identify one stewardship job.
- Lab/application: station setup, task rehearsal, and reset practice.
- Cleanup/reset: station inspection for tools, sanitizer, towels, trash, and floor.
- Exit check: explain your station's top three jobs.

## Day 3: Ticket Flow and Communication Standards
- Opening routine: call-back practice.
- Demo or mini-lesson: clear ticket language, expo communication, and respectful corrections.
- Student work: practice ticket scenarios with call-backs and "heard."
- Lab/application: mock service 2.0 with one reset pause halfway through.
- Cleanup/reset: dish, counter, station, service area, and floor reset.
- Exit check: one phrase or habit that helped the team.

## Day 4: Bistro Prep
- Opening routine: prep huddle.
- Demo or mini-lesson: using a prep list, checking completion, labeling, and leaving prep usable for service.
- Student work: complete assigned prep, station setup, and storage/reset tasks.
- Lab/application: Bistro prep block with teacher checkpoints.
- Cleanup/reset: label, store, clean, reset.
- Exit check: what is ready for service?

## Day 5: Bistro Service
- Opening routine: readiness check, huddle, roles confirmed.
- Demo or mini-lesson: final service reminders for hospitality, timing, communication, and clean-as-you-go.
- Student work: run controlled service in assigned roles.
- Lab/application: service, dish flow, guest awareness, reset, debrief.
- Cleanup/reset: full room reset before reflection.
- Exit check: one improvement from Week 4 and one stewardship habit to keep.
