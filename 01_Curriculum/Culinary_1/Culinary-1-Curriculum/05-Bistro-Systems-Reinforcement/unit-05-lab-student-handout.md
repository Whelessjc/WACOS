# Unit 05 Lab Student Handout: Bistro Systems Practice

## Purpose of the Lab
The purpose of this lab is to improve Bistro systems after the soft opening. You will practice station roles, ticket flow, communication, prep, service, and reset.

## Skills Practiced
- Service debrief
- Station retraining
- Ticket communication
- Mock service 2.0
- Bistro prep
- Guest awareness
- Cleanup and reset

## Equipment Needed
- Tickets or order slips
- Station tools
- Service tools
- Prep tools
- Towels
- Sanitizer and dish supplies

## Ingredients / Materials
Your teacher will choose the simple Bistro prep or service items.

## Mise en Place / Setup Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Role assigned
- [ ] Improvement target named
- [ ] Station tools gathered
- [ ] Prep list checked
- [ ] Tickets ready
- [ ] Sanitizer and towels ready
- [ ] Dish path clear
- [ ] Reset plan clear

## Procedure
1. Complete the entry routine and huddle.
2. Review what happened during the soft opening.
3. Confirm station roles and communication expectations.
4. Practice ticket flow or run mock service 2.0.
5. Complete assigned Bistro prep or service task.
6. Use mid-service reset if directed.
7. Clean and reset fully.
8. Debrief what improved and what needs more practice.

## Teamwork Expectations
- Use call-backs.
- Stay in your role.
- Help before the station falls behind.
- Keep guest experience in mind.
- Talk about systems, not blame.
- Share cleanup and reset.

## Safety Reminders
- Walk during service.
- Keep expo and dish areas clear.
- Use "hot," "behind," and "corner."
- Report spills immediately.
- Keep stations clean and sanitized.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Tools returned
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Dishes routed correctly
- [ ] Ingredients labeled or stored as directed
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Service area reset
- [ ] Station ready for the next group

## Bistro Connection
This lab is direct Bistro practice. Clear roles, tickets, prep, dish flow, and reset make service calmer and more useful for guests.

## Reflection Questions
1. What did your station improve?
2. What communication did you use?
3. What system still needs practice?
4. How did your work affect the guest or team?
5. What reset job did you complete?
