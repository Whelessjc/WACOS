# Unit 05: Bistro Systems Reinforcement

## Weeks Covered
Week 5

## Focus
Stabilize service before moving deeper into technical skills.

## Purpose
Students debrief the soft opening, clarify roles, retrain stations, practice ticket flow and communication standards, complete mock service 2.0, and continue Bistro prep and service. The week also reinforces guest awareness, clean stations, dish flow, and shared stewardship so service feels calmer and more respectful.

## Learning Targets
- I can identify what worked and what needs improvement after service.
- I can explain my station role with more clarity.
- I can use communication standards during ticket flow.
- I can complete prep and reset tasks for Bistro service.
- I can notice how my work affects the guest and the team.
- I can improve one service habit based on feedback.

## Core Skills
- Service debrief
- Role clarity
- Station retraining
- Ticket flow
- Communication standards
- Hospitality and guest awareness
- Bistro prep and reset

## Vocabulary
| Term | Student-Friendly Meaning |
|---|---|
| Debrief | A review of what happened and what to improve. |
| Retraining | Practicing a system again to make it stronger. |
| Role clarity | Knowing exactly what your job is. |
| Call-back | Repeating key information so the team knows it was heard. |
| Reset | Returning the station or room to ready condition. |
| Service standard | The expected way to complete a service task. |

## Lab Applications
- Soft opening debrief
- Station retraining
- Mock service 2.0
- Bistro prep
- Bistro service

## Bistro Connection
This unit keeps Bistro service from becoming chaotic by strengthening the systems students already tried in Week 4: clear roles, clean stations, guest awareness, dish flow, and calm communication.

## Assessment Ideas
- Service debrief participation
- Station retraining check
- Mock service 2.0 observation
- Prep list completion
- Reset inspection

## Teacher Notes
Avoid adding complexity. This week is about tightening service habits before knife skills take more attention. Keep asking, "Would this station be useful to the next person?"

## Resources Needed
- Equipment: station tools, order slips, service tools, towels, sanitizer, dish area
- Ingredients: simple Bistro prep items
- Materials: station role notes, communication expectations, prep list
