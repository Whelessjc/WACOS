# Assessment Notes: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Skill inventory review
- Prep list conference
- Readiness conversation
- Reflection prompt
- Limited role observation
- Final stewardship/reset observation
- Service-readiness communication observation

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by creating a realistic prep list, completing a limited role with appropriate independence, maintaining standards, completing reset, and reflecting with evidence.

## Observation Points
- Can the student name specific skills?
- Does the prep list make sense?
- Does the student take ownership?
- Does the student maintain safety and cleanup?
- Is reflection honest and useful?
- Does the student leave the space ready for the next group?
- Can the student explain what is done, what is waiting, and what needs help?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Reflection | Names specific growth and next steps. |
| Planning | Creates useful prep list. |
| Ownership | Completes role responsibly. |
| Service Readiness | Communicates status and next steps. |
| Professional Habits | Maintains safety, sanitation, teamwork. |
| Reset | Leaves space ready. |
| Evidence | Supports readiness claims with real examples. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can explain what they can do, what they still need to practice, and how they would handle more responsibility in Culinary 2.
