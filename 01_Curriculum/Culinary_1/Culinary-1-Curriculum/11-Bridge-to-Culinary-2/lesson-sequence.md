# Lesson Sequence: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Day 1: Skill Inventory
- Opening routine: huddle on growth and real kitchen habits.
- Demo or mini-lesson: how to name specific skills.
- Student work: complete skill inventory with evidence from labs, service, cleanup, and teamwork.
- Lab/application: quick station or tool identification review.
- Cleanup/reset: room reset.
- Exit check: one skill I can do better now and where I used it.

## Day 2: Independent Prep List
- Opening routine: agenda and task preview.
- Demo or mini-lesson: building a prep list from a simple task.
- Student work: create independent prep list with tools, ingredients, timing, safety, and cleanup.
- Lab/application: prep list conference or small prep task.
- Cleanup/reset: tools returned and station reset.
- Exit check: one thing I planned before acting.

## Day 3: Limited Service Ownership
- Opening routine: role huddle.
- Demo or mini-lesson: what ownership looks like before, during, and after service.
- Student work: take assigned prep or service role.
- Lab/application: limited service ownership task.
- Cleanup/reset: station reset, dish/towel/floor check, and debrief.
- Exit check: how did I support the team?

## Day 4: Reflection and Readiness Conversation
- Opening routine: quiet reflection setup.
- Demo or mini-lesson: honest self-assessment.
- Student work: reflect on strengths, habits, safety, stewardship, and next steps.
- Lab/application: teacher/student readiness conversations as possible.
- Cleanup/reset: materials and room reset.
- Exit check: one habit I need for Culinary 2.

## Day 5: Course Closure and Transition
- Opening routine: final huddle.
- Demo or mini-lesson: Culinary 2 expectations and continued growth through real work.
- Student work: complete final reflection or conversation.
- Lab/application: simple review task, service support, or final reset as schedule allows.
- Cleanup/reset: full course reset with stewardship standard.
- Exit check: one way I am more capable now.
