# Lab Plan: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Lab Purpose
Give students a practical way to show independence, prep planning, service ownership, and reflection.

## Skill Focus
- Skill inventory
- Prep list creation
- Limited station ownership
- Service or prep responsibility
- Reflection
- Final stewardship/reset

## Ingredients or Materials
- Teacher-selected simple prep/service ingredients
- Skill inventory prompts
- Prep list format
- Reflection prompts

## Equipment
- Depends on selected review task
- Station tools
- Towels and sanitizer
- Storage containers if needed

## Setup
- Choose a simple task students can plan.
- Assign limited ownership roles.
- Keep expectations clear and realistic.
- Prepare time for reflection or conversation.

## Procedure
1. Complete opening routine.
2. Review task and expectations.
3. Student creates prep list.
4. Teacher checks plan.
5. Student completes limited prep/service role.
6. Student cleans and resets to the standard expected for another group.
7. Student reflects on readiness and next steps using evidence.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- Prep list is realistic.
- Student works with appropriate independence.
- Safety and sanitation continue.
- Role is completed.
- Reflection uses evidence.
- Final reset is complete without repeated reminders.

## Cleanup and Reset Expectations
Final-week work still requires full reset: tools returned, counters sanitized, dishes done, storage checked, floors checked.

## Possible Variations
- Use a no-cook prep planning task.
- Use a simple Bistro support role.
- Use individual conferences instead of full lab if schedule is tight.
