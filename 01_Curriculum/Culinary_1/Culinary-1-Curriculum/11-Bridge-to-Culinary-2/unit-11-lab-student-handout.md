# Unit 11 Lab Student Handout: Readiness and Limited Ownership

## Purpose of the Lab
The purpose of this lab is to show independence in a realistic, limited way. You will plan a task, complete a role, reset the station, and reflect using evidence.

## Skills Practiced
- Skill inventory
- Prep list creation
- Limited station ownership
- Service or prep responsibility
- Reflection
- Final stewardship and reset

## Equipment Needed
Your teacher will choose equipment based on the review task.

Possible equipment:
- Station tools
- Prep tools
- Towels
- Sanitizer and dish supplies
- Storage containers if needed

## Ingredients / Materials
Your teacher may assign simple prep or service ingredients, skill inventory prompts, prep list format, and reflection prompts.

## Mise en Place / Setup Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Task understood
- [ ] Prep list started
- [ ] Tools listed
- [ ] Ingredients or materials listed
- [ ] Safety concerns named
- [ ] Cleanup/reset plan included
- [ ] Teacher checkpoint complete

## Procedure
1. Complete the entry routine.
2. Review the task and expectations.
3. Create a prep list.
4. Check the plan with the teacher.
5. Complete the limited prep or service role.
6. Clean and reset to the class standard.
7. Complete reflection using evidence from the course.

## Teamwork Expectations
- Own your role.
- Ask for help when needed.
- Communicate clearly.
- Do not leave cleanup for someone else.
- Respect that students may have different next steps.

## Safety Reminders
- Keep handwashing and sanitation habits.
- Use equipment only as approved.
- Keep stations clean while working.
- Report spills or problems.
- Do not rush final reset.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Tools returned
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Dishes handled correctly
- [ ] Storage checked
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station ready for the next group
- [ ] I completed my part of the reset

## Bistro Connection
Future Bistro work depends on independence. A ready student can plan, communicate, complete a role, and reset without waiting to be chased.

## Reflection Questions
1. What did you plan before acting?
2. What role did you own?
3. What evidence shows growth?
4. What habit still needs practice?
5. What responsibility are you ready for next?
