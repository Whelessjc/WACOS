# Student Overview: Bridge to Culinary 2

## What You Are Learning
You will look back at what you learned in Culinary 1 and think about what kind of kitchen responsibility you are ready for next. The goal is to name real evidence from labs, service, cleanup, and teamwork.

## Why It Matters
Growth only helps if you can name it and show it. This week is about knowing your strengths, your habits, your safety responsibilities, and your next steps.

## What Success Looks Like
- I can name skills I improved.
- I can name habits I still need to strengthen.
- I can make a simple prep list.
- I can take ownership of a small role.
- I can explain what Culinary 2 readiness means.
- I can leave a station or kitchen space ready for the next group.

## Applications
Skill inventory, independent prep list, limited service ownership, reflection, and readiness conversation.

## Bottom Line
The goal is to leave Culinary 1 more capable, more aware, more responsible, and more honest about your next step.
