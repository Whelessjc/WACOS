# Unit 11 Student Packet: Bridge to Culinary 2

## What We Are Learning
This week we are looking at independence and readiness. You will complete a skill inventory, create a prep list, take limited ownership of a prep or service task, reflect on growth, and talk about Culinary 2 readiness.

## Why We Are Learning It
Culinary 1 is about real kitchen habits. The final week helps you name what you can do now, what you still need to practice, and what kind of responsibility you are ready for next.

## What Success Looks Like
- I can identify skills I improved.
- I can name habits I still need to strengthen.
- I can create a basic prep list.
- I can take ownership of a limited role.
- I can explain what readiness looks like.
- I can leave the kitchen ready for the next group.

## Daily Kitchen Entry Routine
1. Enter calmly.
2. Put belongings away.
3. Check the agenda.
4. Check uniform/readiness.
5. Join the huddle.
6. Wash hands when directed.
7. Begin work only when released.

## Key Expectations
- Be honest.
- Use evidence from real labs and service.
- Plan before acting.
- Follow through on your role.
- Keep standards through the last day.
- Reset without being chased.

## Kitchen Communication
- "My role is..."
- "My prep list says..."
- "I need help"
- "Heard"
- "Reset complete"
- "My evidence is..."

## Safety Expectations
- Keep using handwashing and sanitation habits.
- Use equipment only when approved.
- Keep stations clean during independent work.
- Do not rush final cleanup.
- Report spills and problems right away.

## Readiness Practice Expectations
- Complete your skill inventory with real examples.
- Create a prep list before starting work.
- Take responsibility for a limited task.
- Ask for help when needed.
- Reflect honestly.
- Complete full cleanup and reset.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Tools returned
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Dishes handled correctly
- [ ] Storage checked
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station ready for the next group
- [ ] I did not leave my reset for someone else

## Bistro Connection
Bistro and Culinary 2 require more independence. Readiness means planning work, communicating clearly, following through, and resetting the station without being chased.

## Student Reflection Questions
1. What skill improved most for you?
2. What habit still needs work?
3. What evidence shows you can take more responsibility?
4. What kind of Bistro or Culinary 2 role interests you?
5. What should you practice next?
