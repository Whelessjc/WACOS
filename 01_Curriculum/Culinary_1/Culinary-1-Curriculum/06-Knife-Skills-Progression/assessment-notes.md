# Assessment Notes: Knife Skills Progression

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Board setup check
- Grip and claw observation
- Cut sample review
- Prep list progress check
- Product sorting/storage check
- Prep list and service-readiness check
- Student reflection on control

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by safely setting up, completing assigned cuts with reasonable control, using product for a prep list, and resetting the station.

## Observation Points
- Is the board stable?
- Are fingers protected?
- Does the student slow down when needed?
- Are cuts usable for cooking?
- Does the student clean knife tools safely?
- Does the student protect usable prep from scraps, waste, or contamination?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Safety | Handles knife and station correctly. |
| Technique | Uses grip, claw, and controlled movement. |
| Cut Quality | Produces usable, reasonably consistent cuts. |
| Prep Use | Applies cuts to a prep list or lab. |
| Service Readiness | Prepares product that can support timing and consistency. |
| Stewardship | Sorts, stores, and cleans prep responsibly. |
| Reset | Cleans and stores tools safely. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can work with a knife safely enough to support simple prep without constant correction.
