# Unit 06 Lab Student Handout: Knife Skills Progression

## Purpose of the Lab
The purpose of knife practice is to build safe control and use cuts for real prep. The goal is not speed first. The goal is control, consistency, station organization, and reset.

## Skills Practiced
- Knife safety
- Board setup
- Grip and claw
- Rough chop, slice, dice, mince
- Julienne and batonnet
- Prep list execution
- Sorting prep, scraps, and waste
- Cleanup and reset

## Equipment Needed
- Chef knife
- Cutting board
- Damp towel
- Bowls or containers
- Bench scraper if available
- Sheet pan if needed
- Towels
- Sanitizer and dish supplies

## Ingredients / Materials
Your teacher will assign practice vegetables, potatoes, soup base vegetables, stir fry vegetables, or roasting vegetables.

## Mise en Place / Setup Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Board stable with damp towel
- [ ] Knife issued correctly
- [ ] Prep list visible
- [ ] Scrap/waste plan clear
- [ ] Usable prep container ready
- [ ] Towel in correct place
- [ ] Teacher demo completed

## Procedure
1. Complete the entry routine and handwashing.
2. Set up board, towel, knife, and containers.
3. Watch the teacher demo.
4. Practice slowly.
5. Pause for grip and claw checkpoint.
6. Complete assigned prep list.
7. Sort usable prep, scraps, and waste.
8. Label or store prep if directed.
9. Clean knife, board, counter, floor, towels, and dishes.

## Teamwork Expectations
- Give each person safe space.
- Keep knives down when talking.
- Help sort prep and scraps.
- Do not rush another student.
- Share cleanup.

## Safety Reminders
- Say "sharp" when moving with a knife as directed.
- Keep claw hand tucked.
- Keep board stable.
- Do not scrape with the knife edge.
- Stop if the station becomes unsafe.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Knife cleaned and stored safely
- [ ] Board washed correctly
- [ ] Counter cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Usable prep stored/labeled as directed
- [ ] Scraps and waste handled
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station ready for the next group

## Bistro Connection
Knife prep feeds Bistro. Clean, consistent, labeled prep helps stations work on time and cook food evenly.

## Reflection Questions
1. What cut did you practice?
2. What helped your control?
3. What correction did you make?
4. How did you handle scraps and usable prep?
5. What reset job did you complete?
