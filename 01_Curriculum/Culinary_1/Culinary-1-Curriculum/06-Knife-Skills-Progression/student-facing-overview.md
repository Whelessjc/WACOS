# Student Overview: Knife Skills Progression

## What You Are Learning
You will practice knife skills over several weeks. You will learn safe setup, grip, claw, basic cuts, and how to use those cuts for real prep.

## Why It Matters
Knife control makes cooking safer and helps food cook evenly. In Bistro work, good prep helps the whole team.

## What Success Looks Like
- I set up my board safely.
- I use grip and claw.
- I cut with control.
- I improve consistency over time.
- I complete prep list tasks.
- I clean and reset my station.

## Applications
Stir fry, sauteed vegetables, soup base, roasted vegetables, potato batonnet, fries, and deep clean reset.

## Bottom Line
Speed comes after control. Safe hands matter more than fast hands.
