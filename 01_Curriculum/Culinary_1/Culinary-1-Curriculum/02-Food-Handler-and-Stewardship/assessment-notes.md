# Assessment Notes: Food Handler and Stewardship

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Handwashing observation
- Sanitizer and counter check
- Dish flow observation
- Allergen scenario response
- Thermometer placement practice
- Stewardship role completion

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by completing a lab while maintaining sanitation, preventing cross-contamination, using dish systems correctly, and checking temperature safely when assigned.

## Observation Points
- Does the student clean without waiting until the end?
- Does the student understand allergen seriousness?
- Does the student keep raw items controlled?
- Does the student use the thermometer correctly?
- Does the student help reset shared spaces?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Hygiene | Washes hands and manages personal cleanliness. |
| Sanitation | Cleans and sanitizes surfaces correctly. |
| Stewardship | Handles dishes, laundry, floors, and storage responsibly. |
| Temperature | Uses thermometer safely and understands why. |
| Team Responsibility | Helps the group protect the kitchen. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can explain and perform basic food safety habits during real kitchen work, not just repeat definitions.
