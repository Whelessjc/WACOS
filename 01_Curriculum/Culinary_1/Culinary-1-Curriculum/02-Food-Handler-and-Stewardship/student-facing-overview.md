# Student Overview: Food Handler and Stewardship

## What You Are Learning
This week you learn how to keep food, tools, classmates, and guests safe. You will practice handwashing, cleaning, sanitizing, dish systems, laundry, storage, floors, allergen awareness, and thermometer use.

## Why It Matters
A kitchen only works when people can trust each other. Food safety is not just rules on paper. It is how you wash, store, clean, cook, and reset every day.

## What Success Looks Like
- I wash hands at the right times.
- I keep raw and ready-to-eat foods separate.
- I treat allergens seriously.
- I clean and sanitize correctly.
- I help with dishes, towels, counters, storage, and floors.
- I use a thermometer correctly when asked.

## This Week's Labs
- Brownie batter
- Baking brownies
- Dish systems training
- Chicken breast temperature lab

## Bottom Line
Safety becomes behavior when you do the right thing even before someone reminds you.
