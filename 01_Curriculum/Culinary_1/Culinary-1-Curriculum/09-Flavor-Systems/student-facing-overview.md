# Student Overview: Flavor Systems

## What You Are Learning
You will learn how cooks build flavor using salt, acid, fat, aromatics, herbs, and spices. You will taste safely, watch for allergens, and make small adjustments.

## Why It Matters
Recipes tell you what to do, but cooks also need to notice flavor and make thoughtful changes.

## What Success Looks Like
- I taste safely.
- I describe flavor with specific words.
- I adjust in small steps.
- I respect cultural origins when comparing flavor builds.
- I can explain what changed after seasoning.
- I clean tasting tools and keep samples safe for others.

## Applications
Soy and ginger, vinegar and herbs, chili and citrus, yogurt and spice, coconut and curry, plus simple balance practice.

## Bottom Line
Flavor improves when cooks taste safely, think, adjust, respect the food, and protect the people eating it.
