# Student Overview: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## What You Are Learning
This week you learn how service works from the moment a guest arrives until the space is reset after they leave.

## Why It Matters
Good food is not enough. A kitchen also needs communication, timing, clean stations, dish flow, and hospitality.

## What Success Looks Like
- I can explain Life of an Order.
- I know my station role.
- I can use respectful service language.
- I can follow ticket flow.
- I can help reset after service.
- I can see how clearing, dishes, and clean stations affect guests.
- I can help take care of both the guest space and kitchen space.

## This Week's Applications
- Station training
- Mock service
- Bistro prep
- Soft opening

## Bottom Line
Service is a team system. Everyone has a job, and every job affects the guest.
