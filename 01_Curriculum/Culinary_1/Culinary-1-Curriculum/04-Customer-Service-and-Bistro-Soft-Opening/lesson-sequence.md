# Lesson Sequence: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Day 1: Life of an Order
- Opening routine: entry, agenda check, huddle.
- Demo or mini-lesson: guest arrival to farewell.
- Student work: trace an order through greeting, ticket, cooking, expo, running, clearing, dish, reset.
- Lab/application: role-play simple service flow.
- Cleanup/reset: guest area, service area, and dish path reset after role-play.
- Exit check: name one place an order can break down.

## Day 2: Station Assignments and Training
- Opening routine: huddle on station ownership.
- Demo or mini-lesson: station duties before, during, after service.
- Student work: learn assigned station expectations.
- Lab/application: station setup and reset practice.
- Cleanup/reset: station inspection for tools, towels, trash, counters, and traffic flow.
- Exit check: what is your station responsible for?

## Day 3: Ticket Flow and Mock Service
- Opening routine: agenda and communication practice.
- Demo or mini-lesson: reading tickets, call-backs, expo basics.
- Student work: practice ticket language.
- Lab/application: mock service with simple orders.
- Cleanup/reset: dish, counters, service area, guest area, and floor reset.
- Exit check: one phrase that helped communication.

## Day 4: Bistro Prep
- Opening routine: huddle with prep goals.
- Demo or mini-lesson: prep list, timing, station readiness.
- Student work: assigned prep and service setup.
- Lab/application: controlled Bistro prep.
- Cleanup/reset: label, store, clean, dish, and reset for the next service.
- Exit check: what prep task supports tomorrow's service?

## Day 5: Soft Opening
- Opening routine: readiness check, huddle, roles confirmed.
- Demo or mini-lesson: final service reminders.
- Student work: run controlled soft opening.
- Lab/application: guest service, ticket flow, expo, running, clearing, dish, reset.
- Cleanup/reset: full service reset.
- Exit check: debrief one success and one system to improve.
