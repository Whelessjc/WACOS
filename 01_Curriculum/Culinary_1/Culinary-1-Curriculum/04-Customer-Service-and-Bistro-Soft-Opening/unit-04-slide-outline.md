# Unit 04 Slide Outline: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Slide 1: Unit 04
- Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening
- Understand the full flow of service
- Real service is a team system

## Slide 2: Why This Unit Matters
- Guests experience the whole room
- Tickets need a clear path
- Stations must work together
- Reset protects the next group

## Slide 3: Life of an Order
- Arrival
- Greeting
- Order and ticket
- Cooking and expo
- Running, clearing, reset, farewell

## Slide 4: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Check agenda and readiness
- Join huddle
- Wash hands when directed
- Wait for release

## Slide 5: Hospitality
- Respectful greeting
- Calm voice
- Guest awareness
- Follow through
- Farewell matters

## Slide 6: Station Roles
- Know your job
- Know your tools
- Know your reset
- Help the team
- Stewardship belongs to every role

## Slide 7: Ticket Flow
- Read the ticket
- Send it to the right place
- Call back key information
- Keep tickets visible

## Slide 8: Expo
- Organizes food before it leaves
- Checks order accuracy
- Keeps the handoff calm
- Limits crowding

## Slide 9: Food Running and Check Back
- Move safely
- Use guest-friendly language
- Confirm the right order
- Notice what the guest needs

## Slide 10: Clearing and Dish
- Clear respectfully
- Move dishes correctly
- Keep dish flow moving
- Watch floors and traffic
- Reset is guest care

## Slide 11: Mock Service
- Practice the system
- Use real communication
- Fix problems early
- Reset after each round

## Slide 12: Stewardship During Service
- Notice what needs doing
- Protect guest spaces
- Protect kitchen spaces
- Do not leave reset for one person

## Slide 13: Soft Opening
- Small and controlled
- Roles confirmed
- Simple menu or task
- Debrief after service

## Slide 14: Safety During Service
- Walk with purpose
- Say "hot" and "behind"
- Keep paths clear
- Report spills

## Slide 15: Cleanup and Reset
- Guest area
- Service area
- Dish area
- Tools, towels, trash, floors

## Slide 16: Exit Reflection
- Where can service break down?
- What role did you understand?
- What system needs practice?
