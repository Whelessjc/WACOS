# Assessment Notes: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Life of an Order explanation
- Station setup check
- Ticket flow practice
- Service language observation
- Guest/service area reset observation
- Mock service debrief

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by completing an assigned station role during mock service or soft opening while communicating, maintaining sanitation, and helping reset.

## Observation Points
- Does the student know the role?
- Does the student communicate status?
- Does the student stay calm when tickets move?
- Does the student notice guest experience?
- Does the student help maintain the guest space and dish flow?
- Does the student help reset?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Hospitality | Greets, responds, and speaks respectfully. |
| Role Clarity | Understands and performs assigned station work. |
| Communication | Uses clear ticket/service language. |
| Sanitation | Keeps station and dish flow controlled. |
| Stewardship | Helps care for guest and kitchen spaces. |
| Reset | Helps return space to ready condition. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can participate in a controlled service without needing constant reminders about role, communication, or reset.
