# Checklist: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Readiness Checklist
- [ ] I know my station role.
- [ ] I know the service flow.
- [ ] I know where tickets go.
- [ ] I know how my station resets.

## Daily Habits
- [ ] Join huddle.
- [ ] Listen for role updates.
- [ ] Use clear communication.
- [ ] Stay in assigned area unless directed.
- [ ] Keep station clean.

## Skill Checks
- [ ] I can explain Life of an Order.
- [ ] I can greet or acknowledge a guest.
- [ ] I can follow ticket flow.
- [ ] I can communicate with expo.
- [ ] I can help clear, dish, and reset.
- [ ] I can notice when the service area needs attention.

## Cleaning and Reset Checks
- [ ] Station reset.
- [ ] Service area reset.
- [ ] Guest area or practice service space reset.
- [ ] Dishes routed.
- [ ] Towels handled.
- [ ] Floor checked.

## Student Self-Check
- [ ] I knew my role.
- [ ] I communicated respectfully.
- [ ] I helped service run smoother.
- [ ] I know one service habit to improve.
