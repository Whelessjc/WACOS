# Lab Plan: Bistro Soft Opening

## Lab Purpose
Practice the full service flow in a controlled way so students understand roles, tickets, hospitality, and reset.

## Skill Focus
- Life of an Order
- Station setup
- Ticket flow
- Expo and food running
- Guest communication
- Dish and reset

## Ingredients or Materials
- Teacher-selected simple Bistro items
- Order slips or tickets
- Station labels if available

## Equipment
- Service station tools
- Prep tools
- Timers
- Towels and sanitizer
- Dish area
- Serving trays or plates as needed

## Setup
- Assign stations.
- Review menu or service item.
- Prepare sample tickets.
- Set clear guest/service boundaries.
- Confirm dish and reset roles.

## Procedure
1. Huddle and confirm roles.
2. Set stations.
3. Practice greeting and order taking.
4. Move tickets through production and expo.
5. Run food or complete service handoff.
6. Check back, clear, dish, and reset.
7. Debrief service flow.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- Greeting is respectful.
- Ticket reaches correct station.
- Expo area stays controlled.
- Students communicate before moving food.
- Dish and reset happen throughout.

## Cleanup and Reset Expectations
Service area, prep stations, dish area, guest area, counters, floors, towels, and tools are reset before dismissal.

## Possible Variations
- Run mock service with staff as guests.
- Use students as practice guests.
- Limit to one or two menu items.
