# Unit 04 Lab Student Handout: Bistro Soft Opening

## Purpose of the Lab
The purpose of the soft opening is to practice service flow in a controlled way. The goal is role clarity, communication, hospitality, dish flow, and reset.

## Skills Practiced
- Greeting and farewell
- Order taking
- Ticket flow
- Station setup
- Expo and food running
- Clearing and dish flow
- Cleanup and reset

## Equipment Needed
- Order slips or tickets
- Station tools
- Service tools or trays as directed
- Towels
- Sanitizer and dish supplies

## Ingredients / Materials
Your teacher will choose the simple Bistro items or service practice materials.

## Mise en Place / Setup Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Role assigned
- [ ] Station tools gathered
- [ ] Tickets or order slips ready
- [ ] Towels and sanitizer ready
- [ ] Dish path clear
- [ ] Guest area ready
- [ ] Reset plan clear

## Procedure
1. Complete the entry routine and huddle.
2. Confirm roles and stations.
3. Set stations before service begins.
4. Practice greeting and order taking.
5. Move tickets through the correct path.
6. Prepare or hand off items as directed.
7. Expo checks orders before they leave.
8. Run food or complete service handoff.
9. Check back, clear, dish, and reset.
10. Debrief what worked and what needs practice.

## Teamwork Expectations
- Use clear service language.
- Stay in your role unless the teacher changes it.
- Help dish and reset before the station falls behind.
- Speak respectfully to guests and teammates.
- Fix systems, not people.

## Safety Reminders
- Walk, do not run.
- Use "behind," "hot," and "corner."
- Keep expo and dish areas from crowding.
- Report spills immediately.
- Keep hands and stations clean.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Guest area reset
- [ ] Service area reset
- [ ] Dishes routed correctly
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Tools returned
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station ready for the next group

## Bistro Connection
This lab is the first controlled Bistro service. The same habits will keep showing up: prep, tickets, communication, guest awareness, dish, and reset.

## Reflection Questions
1. What was your role?
2. What helped ticket flow?
3. Where did service slow down?
4. How did your group show hospitality?
5. What reset habit should we improve next time?
