# Unit 04 Lab Teacher Guide: Bistro Soft Opening

## Setup Before Class
- Choose one or two simple service items or practice tasks.
- Prepare order slips or sample tickets.
- Assign stations and roles visibly.
- Set guest/service boundaries.
- Stage station tools, service tools, towels, sanitizer, and dish supplies.
- Confirm dish flow, trash, towels, floor checks, and final reset plan.

## Demo Points
- Walk through the Life of an Order.
- Model a greeting, order taking, and farewell.
- Show how a ticket moves.
- Show expo expectations.
- Show what a reset table, station, and dish area look like.
- Show how clearing, dish, and reset are part of hospitality.

## Safety Watch-Outs
- Students rushing during service.
- Crowding expo or dish.
- Hot food moving without communication.
- Wet floors near dish or service areas.
- Students forgetting handwashing during role changes.

## Skill Checkpoints
- Students can state their role.
- Ticket reaches the right station.
- Call-backs are clear.
- Expo stays organized.
- Dish flow starts before the end.
- Service area and guest area reset fully.
- Students share stewardship instead of leaving it to one person.

## Common Student Mistakes
- Forgetting to greet or say farewell.
- Losing or ignoring tickets.
- Crowding around expo.
- Treating dish as someone else's job.
- Resetting only the visible guest area.
- Debriefing with blame instead of facts.
- Forgetting that stewardship includes guest space, kitchen space, and dish flow.

## Cleanup Timing
- Pause after mock rounds for quick reset.
- Start dish flow early.
- Assign students to guest area, service area, dish, counters, towels, trash, and floor.
- Inspect before debrief.
- Require incomplete stations to fix reset immediately.

## What to Look For While Circulating
- Are students using role language?
- Are tickets visible and moving?
- Is expo controlled?
- Are students speaking respectfully?
- Is dish flow active?
- Is reset happening throughout service?
- Are students noticing stewardship needs without being chased?

## Bistro Connection
Name the connection clearly: Bistro service is cooking plus hospitality, communication, dish flow, and reset. The soft opening should be small enough for students to see the whole system.

## Quick Debrief Prompts
- Where did the order move smoothly?
- Where did it slow down?
- What did guests or practice guests notice?
- What station needs retraining next week?
