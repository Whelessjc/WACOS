## Unit Connection
Unit 10: Protein as Application

## Purpose
Apply food safety, mise en place, heat control, seasoning, doneness, resting, slicing, and thermometer use to a
teacher-approved protein task.

## Skills Practiced
- raw/clean station setup
- seasoning
- searing
- doneness
- resting
- slicing
- thermometer use
- protein-station reset

## Safety Reminders
- Keep raw and ready-to-eat items separate.
- Wash hands after raw protein contact.
- Use thermometers as evidence.
- Clean probe before and after use.
- Sanitize raw-contact surfaces fully.

## Equipment
- skillet or approved cooking equipment
- tongs/spatula
- thermometer
- cutting board/tray as directed
- sanitizer/probe wipes
- clean landing zone

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Set up raw and clean zones.
2. Season or prepare protein as directed.
3. Cook using teacher-approved method.
4. Check doneness with thermometer when required.
5. Rest and slice if directed.
6. Record evidence or observation.
7. Complete protein-station reset.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- [ ] Raw/clean separation is clear.
- [ ] Thermometer use is correct.
- [ ] Protein is not judged by color only.
- [ ] Resting/slicing is done safely.
- [ ] Raw-contact reset is complete.
