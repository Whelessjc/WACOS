## Unit Connection
Unit 06: Knife Skills Progression

## Purpose
Practice minimum competence with knife safety, board setup, grip, claw, blade path, and useful cuts for cooking.

## Skills Practiced
- knife safety
- board setup
- grip
- claw
- blade path
- rough chop
- dice
- slice
- mince
- julienne or batonnet
- control before speed

## Safety Reminders
- Knives stay down during directions.
- Use a stable board with damp towel.
- Keep claw hand tucked.
- Eyes stay on the blade path.
- Report cuts immediately.

## Equipment
- chef knife or teacher-approved knife
- cutting board
- damp towel
- prep containers
- scrap/compost/trash container

## Ingredients or Materials
Use teacher-approved ingredients, products, and quantities. Do not start until the teacher confirms the exact materials
for your station.

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Set up board, towel, knife, and containers.
2. Watch the teacher demo.
3. Practice slowly with control.
4. Pause for grip, claw, and board checkpoint.
5. Complete assigned cut or prep list.
6. Sort usable product as directed.
7. Clean knives, boards, counters, floors, and containers.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- [ ] Board is stable.
- [ ] Grip and claw are safe.
- [ ] Blade path is controlled.
- [ ] Cut product is organized for cooking or storage.
- [ ] Knife is cleaned and stored correctly.
