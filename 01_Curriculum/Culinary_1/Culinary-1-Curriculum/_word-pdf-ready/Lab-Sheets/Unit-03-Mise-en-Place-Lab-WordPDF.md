## Unit Connection
Unit 03: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## Purpose
Practice reading before cooking, sequencing work, measuring accurately, and setting a station before action.

## Skills Practiced
- recipe reading
- ingredient list reading
- equipment list reading
- step sequencing
- prep lists
- measuring
- mise en place

## Safety Reminders
- Review allergens before handling ingredients.
- Ask before using equipment.
- Keep station organized to prevent spills and cross contact.
- Wash hands when directed.

## Equipment
- recipe copy
- measuring tools
- mixing bowls or prep containers
- station towel
- teacher-selected lab equipment

## Ingredients or Materials
Use teacher-approved ingredients, products, and quantities. Do not start until the teacher confirms the exact materials
for your station.

- Teacher-selected lab ingredients for peanut butter bars, rice cookery, fried rice, or stir fry: [quantity placeholders]

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Read the entire recipe or task.
2. Mark ingredients, equipment, timing, and teacher checkpoints.
3. Build a short prep list.
4. Gather tools before ingredients.
5. Measure ingredients as directed.
6. Set mise en place before mixing or cooking.
7. Complete the lab task only after teacher checkpoint.
8. Clean and reset station.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- [ ] Recipe is marked before action.
- [ ] Prep list includes tools, ingredients, timing, and cleanup.
- [ ] Measuring is accurate.
- [ ] Station is ready before cooking.

## Cleanup / Reset Checklist
- [ ] Dishes routed correctly.
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized.
- [ ] Tools returned clean and dry.
- [ ] Towels/laundry handled correctly.
- [ ] Trash checked.
- [ ] Floor checked.
- [ ] Food stored or discarded as directed.
- [ ] Station ready for the next group.
