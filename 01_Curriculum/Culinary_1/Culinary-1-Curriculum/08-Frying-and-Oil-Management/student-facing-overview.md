# Student Overview: Frying and Oil Management

## What You Are Learning
You will learn how to work safely around hot oil and how temperature affects fried food.

## Why It Matters
Frying can make food crisp and useful for service, but only when cooks control temperature, batch size, tools, and cleanup.

## What Success Looks Like
- I follow oil safety rules.
- I use small batches.
- I avoid overcrowding.
- I drain and season correctly.
- I help keep the oil station clean and safe.
- I watch floors, towels, and tools so the station stays safe.

## Applications
Potato batonnet, fries, vegetable fritters, onion rings, or similar low-cost items.

## Bottom Line
Frying is about temperature discipline, not just putting food in oil.
