# Student Overview: Protein as Application

## What You Are Learning
You will apply safety, sanitation, seasoning, heat control, temperature checking, resting, and slicing to simple protein cooking.

## Why It Matters
Protein can make people sick if handled poorly and can become dry or unsafe if cooked poorly. This unit pulls together many skills from the course.

## What Success Looks Like
- I prevent cross-contamination.
- I use a thermometer correctly.
- I control heat.
- I season with purpose.
- I rest and slice when needed.
- I clean and reset carefully.
- I keep raw and clean items separate.

## Applications
Chicken breast or thigh, ground turkey, eggs, searing, doneness, resting, and slicing.

## Bottom Line
Protein tests whether your safety, heat, knife, organization, and cleanup habits are working.
