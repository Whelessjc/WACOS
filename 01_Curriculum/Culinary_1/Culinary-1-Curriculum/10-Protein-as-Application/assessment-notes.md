# Assessment Notes: Protein as Application

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Sanitation observation
- Thermometer practice
- Heat-control conference
- Doneness cue question
- Resting/slicing observation
- Raw/clean station reset check
- Protein timing/communication observation

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by safely preparing a simple protein, checking doneness correctly, using heat control, resting/slicing as appropriate, and resetting raw/clean areas of the station.

## Observation Points
- Does the student prevent cross-contamination?
- Does the student use the thermometer correctly?
- Does the student manage heat?
- Does the student rest or slice appropriately?
- Does the student sanitize thoroughly?
- Does the student protect finished food from raw-contact tools?
- Does the student communicate doneness, resting, or readiness clearly?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Safety | Controls raw protein and sanitation. |
| Temperature | Checks doneness correctly. |
| Heat Control | Uses appropriate heat and timing. |
| Technique | Seasons, rests, and slices appropriately. |
| Service Readiness | Communicates timing, temperature, and readiness. |
| Reset | Sanitizes and resets fully. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can apply earlier course fundamentals to protein work without creating safety problems.
