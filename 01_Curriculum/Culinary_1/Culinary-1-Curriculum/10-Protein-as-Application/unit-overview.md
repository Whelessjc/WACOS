# Unit 10: Protein as Application

## Weeks Covered
Weeks 16 to 17

## Focus
Protein tests fundamentals.

## Purpose
Students apply safety, sanitation, stewardship, knife control, heat management, doneness, resting, seasoning, slicing, and temperature checking to chicken breast or thigh, ground turkey, egg cookery, searing, and other simple protein applications.

## Learning Targets
- I can handle protein safely and prevent cross-contamination.
- I can season protein with purpose.
- I can use heat control for searing or gentle cooking.
- I can check temperature correctly when required.
- I can rest and slice protein appropriately.
- I can connect protein work to earlier course skills.
- I can reset a protein station so it is safe for the next group.

## Core Skills
- Protein sanitation
- Temperature checking
- Doneness awareness
- Searing
- Egg cookery
- Ground turkey cooking
- Resting and slicing
- Raw/clean station reset

## Vocabulary
| Term | Student-Friendly Meaning |
|---|---|
| Protein | Food such as chicken, turkey, eggs, beans, or meat that needs careful handling. |
| Doneness | How fully cooked a food is. |
| Internal temperature | Temperature inside food. |
| Sear | Brown the surface with controlled heat. |
| Rest | Let cooked protein sit before cutting. |
| Carryover cooking | Food keeps cooking after leaving heat. |

## Lab Applications
- Chicken breast or thigh
- Ground turkey
- Egg cookery
- Searing practice
- Temperature checks
- Resting and slicing

## Bistro Connection
Protein work connects directly to guest safety, timing, service quality, and trust. A Bistro kitchen cannot serve protein well without clean stations, accurate temperatures, and calm communication.

## Assessment Ideas
- Protein sanitation observation
- Thermometer check
- Searing and doneness observation
- Resting/slicing check
- Reflection connecting earlier skills

## Teacher Notes
Keep protein work simple and supervised. This is an application unit, not an advanced meat course.

## Resources Needed
- Equipment: thermometers, pans, sheet pans, cutting boards, knives, tongs, towels, sanitizer
- Ingredients: chicken breast or thigh, ground turkey, eggs, seasonings
- Materials: temperature guidelines and sanitation reminders
