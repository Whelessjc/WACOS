# Unit 10 Lab Student Handout: Protein as Application

## Purpose of the Lab
The purpose of protein labs is to apply the fundamentals you have practiced all semester: sanitation, knife control, heat control, seasoning, timing, thermometer use, teamwork, and reset.

## Skills Practiced
- Protein safety
- Cross-contamination prevention
- Seasoning
- Searing and heat control
- Thermometer use
- Resting and slicing
- Raw/clean cleanup and reset

## Equipment Needed
- Thermometer
- Cutting board
- Knife
- Tongs
- Pan or sheet pan
- Towels
- Sanitizer and dish supplies

## Ingredients / Materials
Your teacher may assign chicken breast or thigh, ground turkey, eggs, basic seasonings, and cooking fat as needed.

## Mise en Place / Setup Checklist
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Raw area identified
- [ ] Clean area identified
- [ ] Tools gathered
- [ ] Thermometer ready
- [ ] Sanitizer ready
- [ ] Towel and dish plan clear
- [ ] Teacher checkpoint before raw protein work

## Procedure
1. Complete the entry routine and protein safety huddle.
2. Set up raw and clean areas.
3. Season and prepare protein as directed.
4. Cook with teacher checkpoints.
5. Check internal temperature when required.
6. Clean thermometer probe correctly.
7. Rest and slice if directed.
8. Evaluate doneness and texture.
9. Sanitize, clean, and reset fully.

## Teamwork Expectations
- Keep raw and clean tasks clear.
- Communicate before moving hot pans or raw tools.
- Call for teacher checkpoints.
- Share cleanup.
- Do not move forward if sanitation is unclear.

## Safety Reminders
- Wash hands after touching raw protein.
- Keep raw tools away from cooked food.
- Clean thermometer probes before and after use.
- Use hot pan safety.
- Use knife safety when slicing.
- Sanitize fully before leaving.

## Cleanup and Reset Checklist
- [ ] Raw-contact surfaces sanitized
- [ ] Thermometers cleaned and returned
- [ ] Boards washed correctly
- [ ] Knives handled safely
- [ ] Raw and clean tools separated and washed
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Towels handled correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station safe for the next group

## Bistro Connection
Protein work connects to guest trust. Food must be handled safely, cooked with evidence, timed well, and served from a clean station.

## Reflection Questions
1. What raw/clean boundary did your group use?
2. What temperature or doneness evidence did you check?
3. What heat adjustment mattered?
4. What earlier unit skill showed up today?
5. What reset step protected the next group?
