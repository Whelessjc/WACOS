# Lab Plan: Protein as Application

## Lab Purpose
Use simple proteins to apply the core Culinary 1 fundamentals in a controlled way.

## Skill Focus
- Protein safety
- Cross-contamination prevention
- Seasoning
- Searing and heat control
- Thermometer use
- Resting and slicing
- Raw/clean station reset

## Ingredients or Materials
- Chicken breast or thigh
- Ground turkey
- Eggs
- Basic seasonings
- Cooking fat as needed

## Equipment
- Thermometers
- Cutting boards
- Knives
- Tongs
- Pans
- Sheet pans
- Towels and sanitizer

## Setup
- Separate raw protein areas.
- Stage thermometers and sanitizer.
- Assign roles.
- Review temperature expectations.

## Procedure
1. Complete handwashing and station setup.
2. Review protein safety.
3. Season and prepare protein.
4. Cook with teacher checkpoints.
5. Check internal temperature when required.
6. Complete a sanitation checkpoint before handling finished food.
7. Rest and slice if appropriate.
8. Evaluate doneness and texture.
9. Clean and reset fully.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- Raw protein contained.
- Thermometer used and cleaned correctly.
- Heat level controlled.
- Doneness verified.
- Station sanitized after protein work.
- Raw tools and clean tools do not cross over.

## Cleanup and Reset Expectations
Raw protein surfaces sanitized, thermometers cleaned, boards washed, knives handled safely, towels managed, floors checked, trash handled.

## Possible Variations
- Use eggs for lower-risk heat review.
- Use ground turkey for small-group sanitation practice.
- Teacher-demo chicken if class readiness is uneven.
