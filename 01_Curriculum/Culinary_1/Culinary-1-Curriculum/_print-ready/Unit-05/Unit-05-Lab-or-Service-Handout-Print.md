## Purpose
Mock service 2.0 is a practice service used to fix what broke down during the soft opening.

The goal is not speed. The goal is clearer roles, stronger ticket flow, better timing, better communication, safer movement, active dish flow, and a better reset.

## What Students Should Practice
- Staying in assigned roles
- Reading tickets clearly
- Calling back key information
- Keeping tickets visible
- Asking for help before timing breaks down
- Moving safely
- Supporting dish flow
- Resetting during service
- Debriefing with facts
- Thinking about guest impact

## What Teachers Are Watching For
- Do students know their roles?
- Are tickets moving to the right station?
- Are students using clear communication?
- Is expo controlled?
- Are dish and reset active?
- Are students fixing problems without blame?
- Is the room calmer than the soft opening?

## Common Breakdowns
- Ticket is unreadable or missing
- Students leave their station without a reason
- Expo gets crowded
- Timing is not communicated early
- Dish piles up
- Towels or floors become unsafe
- Students wait too long to ask for help
- Reset waits until the end

## How To Recover
1. Pause.
2. Keep the station safe.
3. Name the problem.
4. Tell the right person.
5. Fix the next step.
6. Reset what you can.
7. Return to your role.

Recovery should protect the guest experience, the team, and the room.

## Sanitation Reminders
- Wash hands when needed.
- Keep clean and dirty items separated.
- Keep counters clean and sanitized.
- Report spills right away.
- Keep towels and floors controlled.
- Do not let dish flow become invisible.

## Reset Expectations
- Tickets/tasks accounted for
- Guest/service area reset
- Dishes routed correctly
- Counters cleaned and sanitized
- Tools returned
- Ingredients labeled or stored as directed
- Towels handled correctly
- Trash and floors checked
- Station ready for the next group

## Reflection Questions
1. What improved during mock service 2.0?
2. Where did ticket flow slow down?
3. What communication helped your station?
4. What did you do to support dish or reset?
5. What system should we keep practicing?
