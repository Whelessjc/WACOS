## Purpose
Use this handout for Unit 10: Protein tests fundamentals.
The goal is practical kitchen confidence. Work safely, stay organized, use clear words, and reset the station for the next group.
## Key Skills
- chicken breast or thigh
- ground turkey
- egg cookery
- searing
- doneness
- resting
- seasoning
- slicing
- temperature checking
- food safety
- knife control, heat control, mise en place, and flavor balance
## What To Watch For
- Safety before speed.
- Setup before action.
- Small corrections before big problems.
- Clean station before, during, and after work.
- Team communication that helps the task move.
## Bistro Connection
Protein connects to guest safety, timing, doneness, consistency, holding quality, and service readiness. Bistro cannot serve protein well without clean stations and accurate temperature checks.

## Protein Rules That Matter
- Raw and clean do not cross.
- Temperature is evidence, not a guess.
- Resting changes quality.
- Slicing should follow knife safety and the grain/direction shown by the teacher.
- Protein uses previous skills: mise en place, knife control, heat control, seasoning, and sanitation.
