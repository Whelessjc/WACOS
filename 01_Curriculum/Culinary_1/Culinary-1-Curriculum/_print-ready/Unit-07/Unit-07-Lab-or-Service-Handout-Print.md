## Teamwork Expectations
- Stay in your role unless reassigned.
- Use clear, useful communication.
- Ask for help before the station falls behind.
- Share dish, towels, trash, floor, and reset.
## Safety Reminders
- Use dry towels around heat.
- Keep handles turned in.
- Ask before changing heat.
- Watch oil, steam, and hot pans.
- Turn off burners and ovens as directed.

## Heat-Control Checkpoints
- Saute: preheat pan, control oil, use small batches, and avoid steaming.
- Roasting: use spacing, oven control, and color checks.
- Moist heat: watch simmer, liquid ratio, lid use, and texture before stopping.
