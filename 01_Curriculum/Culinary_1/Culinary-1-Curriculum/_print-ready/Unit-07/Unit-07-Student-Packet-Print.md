## What We Are Learning
Learn how cooking actually works.
You will practice:
- saute
- pan preheating
- oil control
- browning vs. steaming
- aromatics without burning
- roasting
- oven control
- spacing
- browning and caramelization
- texture
- moist heat
- rice, beans, and lentils
- simmering
- liquid ratios
## Why We Are Learning It
Heat control helps food cook the way you intend. Preheating, oil control, spacing, simmering, and timing help your team serve food that is safe, consistent, and worth eating.
