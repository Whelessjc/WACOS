## Procedure
1. Complete entry routine and handwashing.
2. Listen to the demo or mini-lesson.
3. Set up the station before starting.
4. Practice the assigned skill at a controlled pace.
5. Pause for teacher checkpoint.
6. Complete the lab or service application.
7. Clean, store, dish, and reset fully.
8. Complete reflection or debrief.
## Teamwork Expectations
- Stay in your role unless reassigned.
- Use clear, useful communication.
- Ask for help before the station falls behind.
- Share dish, towels, trash, floor, and reset.
## Safety Reminders
- Use clean spoons for tasting.
- No double-dipping.
- Name allergens before tasting.
- Keep chili, dairy, soy, coconut, and spice risks organized.
- Sanitize tasting surfaces.
