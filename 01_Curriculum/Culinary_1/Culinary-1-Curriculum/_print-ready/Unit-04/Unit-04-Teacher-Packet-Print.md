## Unit Overview
**Weeks covered:** Week 4
**Focus:** Understand the full flow of service.

Students learn Life of an Order, guest arrival, greeting, order taking, ticket flow, cooking, expo, food running, check back, clearing, dish, reset, farewell, station assignments, mock service, Bistro prep, and soft opening routines.

## Week / Day Flow
- Monday: Life of an Order walkthrough from guest arrival to farewell.
- Tuesday: Station assignments, role expectations, communication, and guest interaction basics.
- Wednesday: Ticket flow, expo, food running, clearing, dish, and reset practice.
- Thursday: Mock service with coaching, stop points, and debrief.
- Friday: Bistro prep and soft opening with controlled expectations.

## Setup Notes
- [ ] Post station assignments and service map.
- [ ] Prepare tickets, menus/tasks, table numbers, expo area, and reset supplies.
- [ ] Decide who may speak to guests and how students get help.
- [ ] Set a simple debrief structure before service.

## Demo Notes
- Walk a sample order through the room.
- Model greeting, order taking, check back, clearing, and farewell.
- Show ticket handoff and expo communication.
- Model what to do when unsure: pause, keep safe, ask, return to role.

## Safety Watch-Outs
- Keep guest areas clear and clean.
- Control hot food movement and traffic paths.
- Keep dish and service items separated.
- Wash hands and reset after contamination.
- Do not let service pressure override sanitation.

## Common Student Mistakes
- Students leave assigned roles without communicating.
- Tickets disappear or are hard to read.
- Expo gets crowded.
- Guest interaction becomes too casual or too quiet.
- Dish and reset are ignored until the end.

## Cleanup / Reset Notes
- Build reset into service, not only after service.
- Check guest area, service area, dish, counters, towels, trash, and floors.
- Debrief without blame: name the system that needs practice.

## Bistro / Service Connection
This unit is the first full service picture. Students see how prep, ticket flow, cooking, expo, dish, reset, and hospitality all connect.
