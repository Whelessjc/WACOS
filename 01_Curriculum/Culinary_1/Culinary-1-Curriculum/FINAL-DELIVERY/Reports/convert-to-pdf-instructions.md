# Convert To PDF Instructions

If local PDF export is not available or needs better formatting, use Google Docs:

1. Upload the Markdown or DOCX file to Google Drive.
2. Open it as a Google Doc.
3. Review page breaks and spacing.
4. Choose `File` > `Download` > `PDF Document`.
5. Save the PDF in the `Printer-Ready-PDFs` folder.

Recommended first exports:
- Student packet
- Checklist bundle
- Lab sheet bundle
- Student quick reference guide
