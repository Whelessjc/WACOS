# Unit 06 Slide Deck Content: Knife Skills Progression


## Slide 1: Unit 06
- Knife Skills Progression
- Weeks 6 to 9
- Control before speed

Teacher note: Frame the unit as safe, repeated practice for real prep.

## Slide 2: Unit Focus
- Build confidence
- Use control
- Repeat core habits
- Prep with purpose

Teacher note: Knife cuts are tools for cooking, not trophies.

## Slide 3: Minimum Competence
- Safe station
- Grip and claw
- Safe blade path
- Controlled pace
- Full reset

Teacher note: Explain that Culinary 1 expects safe, useful beginner knife work.

## Slide 4: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Check agenda
- Join huddle
- Wash hands
- Wait for release

Teacher note: Keep routines steady before knives are issued.

## Slide 5: Knife Safety
- Knife down during directions
- Carry safely
- Keep space
- Use stop signal
- Ask for help

Teacher note: Stop unsafe habits immediately.

## Slide 6: Board Setup
- Damp towel
- Stable board
- Scrap plan
- Prep container
- Clean counter

Teacher note: A stable board comes before any cut.

## Slide 7: Grip and Claw
- Controlled grip
- Fingers tucked
- Knuckles guide
- Eyes on cut
- Slow first

Teacher note: Repeat grip and claw correction often.

## Slide 8: Blade Path
- Tip controlled
- Knife moves forward/down
- Eyes on product
- Product stays organized

Teacher note: Watch for wandering tips and edge scraping.

## Slide 9: Basic Cuts
- Rough chop
- Dice
- Slice
- Mince

Teacher note: Connect each cut to a cooking use.

## Slide 10: Progression Cuts
- Julienne
- Batonnet
- Potato batonnet
- Speed with control

Teacher note: Keep pace slow until control is steady.

## Slide 11: Cut Size Matters
- Cooks faster or slower
- Cooks more evenly
- Changes texture
- Changes browning
- Affects timing

Teacher note: Show how prep quality affects cooking.

## Slide 12: Cooking Applications
- Stir fry
- Sauteed vegetables
- Soup base
- Roasted vegetables
- Fries prep

Teacher note: Use available products and keep applications simple.

## Slide 13: Bistro Connection
- Prep lists
- Batch containers
- Labels
- Timing
- Service readiness

Teacher note: Knife work supports service when it is organized.

## Slide 14: Cleanup and Reset
- Knives safe
- Boards washed
- Counters sanitized
- Prep stored
- Floors checked

Teacher note: Cleanup is part of knife safety.

## Slide 15: Week 9 Deep Clean
- Stations
- Tools
- Storage
- Dish area
- Ready for heat control

Teacher note: Use deep clean to reset before heat control.

## Slide 16: Exit Reflection
- What improved?
- What needs repetition?
- What safety habit worked?

Teacher note: Use as an exit response or quick debrief.
