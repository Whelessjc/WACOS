# Unit 02 Slide Deck: Food Handler and Stewardship

Focus: Safety becomes behavior.

## Slide 1: Unit 02 Focus
- Safety becomes behavior
- Clean hands
- Clean stations
- Safe food
- Shared responsibility

Teacher note:
Connect safety to daily kitchen behavior, not just a test.

## Slide 2: Personal Hygiene
- Wash hands
- Hair controlled
- Safe uniform
- No unsafe jewelry
- Stay home when sick

Teacher note:
Add school-specific expectations out loud.

## Slide 3: When to Wash Hands
- Before food work
- After contamination
- After restroom
- After trash or dishes
- When told to reset

Teacher note:
Have students point to the handwashing sink and name triggers.

## Slide 4: Cross Contamination
- Raw and ready-to-eat
- Clean and dirty
- Allergens
- Phones and faces
- Trash and floors

Teacher note:
Use examples from the actual room.

## Slide 5: Cleaning and Sanitizing
- Clean first
- Sanitize second
- Use as directed
- Let it work
- Do not guess

Teacher note:
Show the difference between wiping and sanitizing.

## Slide 6: Stewardship Jobs
- Laundry
- Counters
- Dishes
- Storage
- Floors

Teacher note:
Frame these as kitchen responsibilities, not punishment.

## Slide 7: Dish Flow
- Scrape
- Sort
- Wash
- Dry
- Put away

Teacher note:
Adapt this to the actual sink or dish machine setup.

## Slide 8: Three-Compartment Sink
- Wash
- Rinse
- Sanitize
- Air dry
- Keep moving

Teacher note:
Use only if this is part of the classroom system.

## Slide 9: Dish Machine
- Rack correctly
- Do not overload
- Watch hot surfaces
- Return clean items
- Keep area clear

Teacher note:
Use only if this is part of the classroom system.

## Slide 10: Time and Temperature
- Use a thermometer
- Check the center
- Clean the probe
- Record as directed
- No guessing

Teacher note:
Connect directly to the chicken temperature lab.

## Slide 11: Brownie Labs
- Clean hands
- Shared tools
- Sticky counters
- Oven safety
- Storage

Teacher note:
Use brownies to practice sanitation and dish flow.

## Slide 12: Chicken Temperature Lab
- Thermometer placement
- Safe reading
- Clean probe
- Record evidence
- Reset raw area

Teacher note:
Follow local food-safety rules for the temperature target.

## Slide 13: Bistro Connection
- Guests depend on us
- Clean counters
- Safe storage
- Active dish flow
- Controlled towels

Teacher note:
Make the service connection practical and specific.

## Slide 14: Exit Reflection
- One safety habit
- One stewardship job
- One reset job
- One question

Teacher note:
Use as a quick written response.
