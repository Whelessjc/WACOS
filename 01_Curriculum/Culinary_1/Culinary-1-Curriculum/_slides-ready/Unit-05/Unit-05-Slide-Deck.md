# Unit 05 Slide Deck Content: Bistro Systems Reinforcement


## Slide 1: Unit 05
- Bistro Systems Reinforcement
- Stabilize service
- Practice the fix

Teacher note: Frame the week as repetition with purpose.

## Slide 2: Why This Unit Matters
- Service needs systems
- Roles reduce confusion
- Tickets need a path
- Reset protects the next team

Teacher note: Connect this week to what students saw during the soft opening.

## Slide 3: Debrief Rules
- Use facts
- No blame
- What worked?
- What slowed down?
- What next?

Teacher note: Keep the debrief focused on systems, not personalities.

## Slide 4: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Check agenda
- Join huddle
- Wash hands
- Wait for release

Teacher note: Keep the routine consistent from earlier units.

## Slide 5: Station Roles
- Setup job
- Service job
- Timing cue
- Ticket/task flow
- Reset job

Teacher note: Each station needs before, during, and after-service clarity.

## Slide 6: Ticket Flow
- Readable ticket
- Correct station
- Call-back
- Expo check
- Completed/reset

Teacher note: Practice with sample tickets before service.

## Slide 7: Communication Standards
- Short
- Clear
- Respectful
- Confirmed
- Useful

Teacher note: Students should use language that helps work move.

## Slide 8: Mock Service 2.0
- Practice the fix
- Use tickets
- Reset midway
- Recover calmly

Teacher note: Use one planned pause to teach recovery.

## Slide 9: Bistro Prep
- Prep list
- What is first?
- Labeling
- Storage
- Station readiness

Teacher note: Prep should make service easier, not create more confusion.

## Slide 10: Guest Awareness
- Guests notice timing
- Guests notice tone
- Guests notice cleanliness
- Guests trust safe systems

Teacher note: Hospitality is practiced behavior.

## Slide 11: Stewardship
- Dishes
- Towels
- Counters
- Floors
- Storage

Teacher note: Stewardship is service work.

## Slide 12: Safety During Service
- Clear paths
- Hot food language
- Spill response
- Dish area control

Teacher note: Service pressure makes basic safety more important.

## Slide 13: Cleanup and Reset
- Tickets accounted for
- Stations reset
- Dish flow complete
- Counters sanitized
- Floors checked

Teacher note: Service is not finished until the room is ready for the next group.

## Slide 14: Exit Reflection
- What improved?
- What still breaks down?
- What habit will you repeat?

Teacher note: Use as an exit ticket or service debrief.
