# Culinary 1 Recipe and Lab Index

This index is a planning tool. It does not finalize recipes or formulas.
Use placeholders until the teacher confirms ingredients, quantities, equipment, food-safety rules, budget, and available products.

## Pancakes
- **Unit connection:** Unit 01
- **Skill focus:** recipe reading, measuring, mise en place, teamwork, heat awareness
- **Approximate difficulty:** Beginner
- **Equipment needed:** mixing bowls, measuring tools, whisk/spoon, griddle or pan, turner
- **Food safety concern:** handwashing, shared tools, hot surface
- **Cleanup/reset concern:** sticky batter, hot pan, dishes, counters, floors
- **Possible Bistro connection:** First low-stakes lab for station setup and reset
- **Notes for future recipe development:** Use teacher-approved pancake mix or recipe; exact formula still needed.
