## Audit Issues Found
- The first version of the recipe index used a wide Markdown table that was hard to scan.
- Exact recipes and quantities are not available yet, so lab sheets needed placeholders instead of invented formulas.
- Several future visuals and diagrams require local classroom details before final design.

## Revisions Made
- Converted the recipe index from a wide table into scan-friendly recipe/lab cards.
- Kept exact ingredients and quantities as placeholders where they are not confirmed.
- Verified all required resource folders and files are present.
- Verified safety, cleanup/reset, stewardship, and Bistro/service language is visible across the resource layer.
- Confirmed no actual images, PDFs, PowerPoint files, websites, or finalized recipes were created.
