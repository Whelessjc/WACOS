Unit 01: Orientation and Systems

## Purpose
Practice recipe reading, measuring, mise en place, teamwork, heat awareness, and full station reset through a simple pancake lab.

## Skills Practiced
- recipe reading
- basic measuring
- mise en place
- station setup
- teamwork
- heat awareness
- cleanup/reset

## Safety Reminders
- Wash hands before food work.
- Use heat only after teacher approval.
- Treat griddles, pans, and spatulas as hot.
- Keep batter and tools at your station.

## Equipment
- mixing bowl
- measuring cups/spoons
- whisk or spoon
- griddle or pan
- turner/spatula
- sanitizer and towels

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Complete entry routine and handwashing.
2. Read the pancake directions as a group.
3. Gather tools and ingredients.
4. Measure ingredients carefully.
5. Mix batter only as directed.
6. Get teacher approval before turning on heat.
7. Cook pancakes while watching bubbles, edges, color, and heat level.
8. Clean as you go and complete full reset.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- [ ] Recipe has been read before ingredients are touched.
- [ ] Measuring tools are correct.
- [ ] Station is set before mixing.
- [ ] Heat is used only after approval.
- [ ] Station reset is complete before dismissal.

## Cleanup / Reset Checklist
- [ ] Dishes routed correctly.
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized.
- [ ] Tools returned clean and dry.
- [ ] Towels/laundry handled correctly.
- [ ] Trash checked.
- [ ] Floor checked.
- [ ] Food stored or discarded as directed.
- [ ] Station ready for the next group.
