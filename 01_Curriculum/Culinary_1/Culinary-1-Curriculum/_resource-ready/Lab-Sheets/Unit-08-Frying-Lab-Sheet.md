## Unit Connection
Unit 08: Frying and Oil Management

## Purpose
Practice oil safety, batch control, draining, seasoning timing, and oil-station reset using a teacher-approved low-cost application.

## Skills Practiced
- oil safety
- batch size
- temperature discipline
- crisp vs. greasy
- draining
- seasoning timing
- potato batonnet, fritters, or onion rings

## Safety Reminders
- Keep water away from hot oil.
- Use tools, not fingers, near oil.
- Limit students at the oil station.
- Stop for spills, crowding, splatter, or unsafe movement.
- Handle oil only as directed.

## Equipment
- fryer or teacher-approved frying setup
- spider/tongs
- rack or draining setup
- sheet pan/tray
- seasoning container
- dry towels

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Review oil safety rules.
2. Set up draining and seasoning area.
3. Confirm product is dry or prepared as directed.
4. Fry in small controlled batches only after teacher approval.
5. Drain correctly and season at the right time.
6. Keep the oil station uncrowded.
7. Complete oil-station reset as directed.

## Teacher Checkpoints
