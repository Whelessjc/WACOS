## Unit Connection
Unit 02: Food Handler and Stewardship

## Purpose
Practice thermometer use and time/temperature control with teacher-approved chicken product.

## Skills Practiced
- food safety
- thermometer placement
- temperature evidence
- probe cleaning
- raw/clean separation
- station reset

## Safety Reminders
- Follow all raw poultry directions from the teacher.
- Keep raw and ready-to-eat items separate.
- Wash hands after raw-product contact.
- Clean thermometer probes before and after use.
- Use the teacher-approved safe temperature standard.

## Equipment
- food thermometer
- sanitizer/probe wipes as directed
- gloves if required
- cutting board or tray as directed
- recording sheet or notebook

## Mise en Place Checklist
- [ ] Read the full lab sheet before starting.
- [ ] Wash hands.
- [ ] Gather tools and materials.
- [ ] Set up towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan.
- [ ] Know your role.
- [ ] Wait for the teacher checkpoint before heat, knives, fryer, or raw protein work.

## Procedure
1. Complete handwashing and station setup.
2. Listen to the thermometer demo.
3. Identify the thickest part of the sample.
4. Insert thermometer as directed without touching bone, pan, or tray if applicable.
5. Read and record the temperature.
6. Clean the probe as directed.
7. Reset raw-contact surfaces and tools.

## Teacher Checkpoints
- [ ] Student can explain where to place thermometer.
- [ ] Temperature is recorded as evidence.
- [ ] Probe cleaning is correct.
- [ ] Raw-contact reset is complete.
