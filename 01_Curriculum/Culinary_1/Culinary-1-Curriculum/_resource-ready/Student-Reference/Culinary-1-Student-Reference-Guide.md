# Culinary 1 Student Reference Guide

Name: ________________________________    Class Period: ________

## Kitchen Entry Routine
- Enter calmly.
- Put belongings where they belong.
- Check the agenda.
- Check uniform/readiness.
- Join the huddle.
- Wash hands when directed.
- Begin only when released.

## Uniform Check
- Hair restrained.
- Safe shoes.
- No loose items.
- Jewelry follows school policy.
- Ready to work before lab starts.

## Handwashing
Wash hands:
- Before food work.
- After restroom.
- After touching face, hair, phone, trash, floor, or dirty dishes.
- After coughing, sneezing, or blowing nose.
- After handling raw food or allergens.
- Any time the teacher calls for a reset.

## Recipe Reading Steps
1. Read the whole recipe or task.
2. Find the ingredients.
3. Find the equipment.
4. Notice timing and wait steps.
5. Look for teacher checkpoints.
6. Ask before guessing.

## Mise en Place Steps
- Recipe or task visible.
- Tools gathered.
- Ingredients gathered and measured.
- Towel, trash, dish, and sanitizer plan ready.
- Team roles clear.
- Station checked before action.

## Knife Safety Reminders
- Knife down during directions.
- Stable board first.
- Use grip and claw.
- Eyes on the blade path.
- Control before speed.
- Report cuts immediately.

## Heat Safety Reminders
- Use dry towels around heat.
- Turn pan handles in.
- Call "hot" when moving hot items.
- Ask before changing heat.
- Watch oil, steam, pans, and ovens.
- Turn off heat as directed.

## Cleanup / Reset Expectations
You are not done when the food is done.

Reset includes:
- Dishes moving correctly.
- Counters cleaned and sanitized.
- Tools returned.
- Towels/laundry handled correctly.
- Trash checked.
- Floors checked.
- Food stored or discarded as directed.
- Station ready for the next group.

## Stewardship Jobs Overview
- Laundry: towels go where directed, not on counters or floors.
- Counters: clean first, then sanitize as directed.
- Dishes: scrape, sort, wash, dry, and put away correctly.
- Storage: close, label if directed, and return items to the right place.
- Floors: report spills right away and sweep/mop as directed.
- Shared spaces: leave the room better for the next group.

## Bistro Service Reminders
- Know your role.
- Keep tickets or tasks visible.
- Use clear words: behind, hot, sharp, corner, heard, order up, I need help.
- Communicate early.
- Keep dish and reset moving.
- Hospitality is practiced behavior: notice, listen, follow through, reset.

## What To Do When Unsure
1. Pause.
2. Keep the station safe.
3. Ask the right person or teacher.
4. Use clear words.
5. Return to the task.
