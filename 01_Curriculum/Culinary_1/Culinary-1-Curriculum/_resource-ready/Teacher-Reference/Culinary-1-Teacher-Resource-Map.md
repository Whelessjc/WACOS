## Suggested Order for Polishing Resources
1. Review the recipe/lab index and confirm which labs will actually be used.
2. Confirm local safety rules for fryer, raw protein, thermometer use, dish machine, and three-compartment sink.
3. Finalize exact recipes and quantities only after teacher review.
4. Polish Unit 01, Unit 02, and Unit 03 lab sheets first.
5. Create high-priority visuals: entry routine, handwashing, stewardship jobs, dish flow, recipe reading, mise en place, knife grip/claw, oil safety, and cleanup/reset.
6. Create high-priority diagrams: Life of an Order, station setup, dish flow, knife grip/claw, fryer safety zone, protein doneness workflow, and kitchen reset workflow.
7. Export a small sample set to PDF/Word or slides before mass-formatting.

## Warnings Before Classroom Use
- Exact recipes and quantities are not finalized in this resource layer.
- Unit 08 frying resources must match local fryer access and oil disposal policy.
- Unit 10 protein resources must match district food-safety guidance and approved products.
- Unit 02 sink/dish machine procedures must match the actual classroom setup.
- Unit 11 readiness language should match the actual Culinary 2 recommendation or enrollment process.
- Visuals and diagrams are plans only; no final images have been created yet.
