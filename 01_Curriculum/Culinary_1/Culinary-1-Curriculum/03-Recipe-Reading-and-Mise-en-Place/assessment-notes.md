# Assessment Notes: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## Formative Assessment Ideas
- Annotated recipe check
- Ingredient/equipment list check
- Prep list review
- Mise en place inspection
- Shared ingredient/tool stewardship check
- Rice ratio question
- End-of-lab reflection

## Performance Assessment Ideas
Students demonstrate readiness by preparing from a simple recipe with organized mise en place, correct measuring, safe teamwork, and full cleanup.

## Observation Points
- Does the student read before acting?
- Does the student understand sequence?
- Does the student gather the right tools?
- Does the student keep the station organized?
- Does the student handle shared tools and ingredients responsibly?
- Does the student adjust when the group gets off track?

## Simple Rubric Categories
| Category | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Recipe Reading | Identifies ingredients, equipment, steps, and yield. |
| Preparation | Builds prep list and mise en place. |
| Measuring | Uses tools carefully and accurately enough. |
| Teamwork | Follows role and communicates. |
| Stewardship | Uses shared tools and ingredients with care. |
| Reset | Cleans and returns station to ready condition. |

## Evidence of Readiness
The student can move from recipe to organized action without needing the teacher to direct every step.
