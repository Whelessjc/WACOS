# Student Overview: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## What You Are Learning
This week you learn how to read a recipe, gather the right tools, organize ingredients, plan steps, and set up before cooking.

## Why It Matters
Good cooks do not guess their way through a recipe. They read, prepare, measure, and organize so the kitchen runs smoothly.

## What Success Looks Like
- I read the whole recipe before starting.
- I can find ingredients, equipment, yield, and steps.
- I can help make a prep list.
- I can set up mise en place.
- I can follow a recipe with my group.
- I can clean and reset after the lab.
- I can keep my station organized while I cook.
- I can take care of shared ingredients and tools.

## This Week's Applications
- Food Handler review and assessment
- Peanut butter bars
- Rice cookery
- Fried rice or stir fry

## Bottom Line
Read before cooking. Organize before acting.
