# Checklist: Heat Control

## Readiness Checklist
- [ ] I know the heat source rules.
- [ ] I have a clear station.
- [ ] I know the cooking method for today.
- [ ] I know what cue to watch for.

## Daily Habits
- [ ] Get approval before using heat.
- [ ] Keep towels away from heat.
- [ ] Communicate "hot."
- [ ] Watch food closely.
- [ ] Adjust heat instead of panicking.
- [ ] Wipe, dish, and reset before the station gets out of control.
- [ ] Communicate timing if food is needed for service or holding.

## Skill Checks
- [ ] Pan preheating
- [ ] Oil control
- [ ] Saute
- [ ] Browning vs. steaming
- [ ] Aromatics without burning
- [ ] Roasting
- [ ] Simmering
- [ ] Liquid ratios
- [ ] Hot-station reset
- [ ] Holding or timing awareness

## Cleaning and Reset Checks
- [ ] Heat source off.
- [ ] Hot equipment handled safely.
- [ ] Dishes handled.
- [ ] Counters sanitized.
- [ ] Floor checked.

## Student Self-Check
- [ ] I noticed at least one heat cue.
- [ ] I made a heat adjustment.
- [ ] I can explain what heat did to the food.
