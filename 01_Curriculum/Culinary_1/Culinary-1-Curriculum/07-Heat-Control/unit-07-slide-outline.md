# Unit 07 Slide Outline: Heat Control

## Slide 1: Unit 07
- Heat Control
- Learn how cooking works
- Watch the food

## Slide 2: Why Heat Matters
- Changes texture
- Builds color
- Affects flavor
- Controls timing

## Slide 3: Daily Routine
- Enter calmly
- Check agenda and readiness
- Join huddle
- Wash hands
- Wait for release

## Slide 4: Hot-Station Safety
- Dry towels
- Handles turned in
- Clear paths
- Report spills
- Heat off as directed
- Hot stations belong to the whole class

## Slide 5: Pan Preheating
- Cold pan
- Ready pan
- Oil shimmer
- Smoke means too hot

## Slide 6: Saute
- Small amount of fat
- Controlled heat
- Small batches
- Food moves with purpose

## Slide 7: Browning vs. Steaming
- Space matters
- Moisture matters
- Patience matters
- Crowding weakens texture

## Slide 8: Aromatics
- Watch smell
- Watch color
- Stir with purpose
- Do not burn the base

## Slide 9: Roasting
- Oven control
- Sheet pan spacing
- Cut size
- Browning and texture

## Slide 10: Moist Heat
- Simmer, not boil
- Liquid ratios
- Rice
- Beans and lentils

## Slide 11: Heat Cues
- Color
- Sound
- Steam
- Smell
- Texture

## Slide 12: Bistro Connection
- Timing
- Holding quality
- Consistency
- Hot-station readiness
- Clean reset for the next task
- Stewardship keeps hot stations safe

## Slide 13: Cleanup and Reset
- Heat off
- Pans safe
- Counters sanitized
- Towels handled
- Floors checked
- Oil/spills handled right away

## Slide 14: Exit Reflection
- What cue did you notice?
- What adjustment did you make?
- What needs more practice?
