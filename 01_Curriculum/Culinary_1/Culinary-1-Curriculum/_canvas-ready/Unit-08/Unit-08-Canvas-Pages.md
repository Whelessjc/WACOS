## Page: Unit Overview
This unit connects classroom directions to real kitchen behavior. You will practice skills that help the room stay
safe, organized, useful, and ready for the next group.

You will learn:
- oil safety
- fryer behavior
- batch size
- overcrowding
- crisp vs. greasy
- draining

## Page: Lab or Service Expectations
Before work starts:
- Read the directions or task.
- Set up tools and materials.
- Know your role.
- Ask before guessing.

During work:
- Communicate early.
- Work at a controlled pace.
- Keep the station clean enough to keep working.
- Support your team.

## Page: Safety and Cleanup
Safety and cleanup are part of the work.

- Wash hands when directed.
- Keep clean and dirty items separate.
- Use tools and equipment only as approved.
- Keep dishes moving.
- Sanitize counters as directed.
- Check towels, trash, floors, storage, and station reset.
- Leave the station ready for the next group.
