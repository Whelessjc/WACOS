# Unit-08 Canvas Module Overview: Frying and Oil Management

## Module Title
Unit-08: Frying and Oil Management

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Temperature discipline.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills, safe
habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- oil safety
- fryer behavior
- batch size
- overcrowding
- crisp vs. greasy
- draining
- seasoning timing
- potato batonnet
- fritters or onion rings

## What You Will Do
- practice oil safety language
- observe or complete controlled frying
- manage batch size
- reset oil station safely

## What You Will Turn In
- frying safety check
- lab reflection
- oil-station reset check

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- frying safety handout
- frying lab handout
- slide deck content
