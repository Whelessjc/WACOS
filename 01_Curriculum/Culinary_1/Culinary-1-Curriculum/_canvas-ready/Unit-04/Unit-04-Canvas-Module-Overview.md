# Unit-04 Canvas Module Overview: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Module Title
Unit-04: Customer Service and Bistro Soft Opening

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Understand the full flow of service.** This unit is about real cooking, real
skills, safe habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- Life of an Order
- greeting
- order taking
- ticket flow
- expo
- food running
- check back
- clearing
- dish
- reset
- soft opening

## What You Will Do
- walk through an order
- practice station roles
- run mock service
- participate in soft opening

## What You Will Turn In
- Life of an Order response
- station role check
- soft opening reflection

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- Life of an Order handout
- soft opening handout
- slide deck content
