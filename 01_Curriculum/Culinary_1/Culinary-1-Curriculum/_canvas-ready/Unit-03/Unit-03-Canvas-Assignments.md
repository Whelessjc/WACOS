# Unit-03 Canvas Assignments: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## Assignment 1: Unit Checklist Completion
**Student instructions:** Complete the unit checklist during the week. Use it to track readiness, safety,
cleanup/reset, and your role in the kitchen.

**Submission type recommendation:** Text entry, file upload, or paper check by teacher.

**Point value suggestion:** 10 points

**Success criteria:**
- Checklist is complete.
- Student can explain one safety or reset habit.
- Student uses the checklist during class, not only after class.

**Teacher notes:** Use this as a quick accountability check, not a long grading task.

## Assignment 2: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place Prep
**Student instructions:** Before this unit task, answer:
- What are we doing today?
- What tools or materials do you need?
- What safety habit matters most?
- What cleanup/reset job will your group need to finish?

**Submission type recommendation:** Canvas text entry or notebook check.

**Point value suggestion:** 10 points

**Success criteria:**
- Answers are specific to the day's task.
- Student names a real safety habit.
- Student includes cleanup/reset.

**Teacher notes:** This can be used as an entry ticket before students move to stations.

## Assignment 3: Unit Reflection
**Student instructions:** After the main lab or service work, write 4 to 6 sentences about what you practiced and
what evidence you have from class.

**Submission type recommendation:** Canvas text entry.

**Point value suggestion:** 15 points

**Success criteria:**
- Names one skill from the unit.
- Gives one real example from lab or service.
- Explains one safety, stewardship, or reset habit.
- Connects the work to Bistro/service when useful.

**Teacher notes:** Look for practical evidence, not perfect wording.
