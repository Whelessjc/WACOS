# Unit-03 Canvas Module Overview: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## Module Title
Unit-03: Recipe Reading and Mise en Place

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Read before cooking. Organize before acting.** This unit is about real cooking,
real skills, safe habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- recipe structure
- ingredients and equipment
- sequencing
- prep lists
- measuring
- mise en place
- rice cookery
- peanut butter bars
- stir fry or fried rice

## What You Will Do
- mark up a recipe
- build a prep list
- measure carefully
- set mise en place before lab

## What You Will Turn In
- recipe-reading practice
- prep list
- mise en place lab reflection

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- recipe-reading handout
- mise en place lab handout
- slide deck content
