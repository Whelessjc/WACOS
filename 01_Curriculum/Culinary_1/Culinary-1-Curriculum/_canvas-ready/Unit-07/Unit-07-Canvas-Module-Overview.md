# Unit-07 Canvas Module Overview: Heat Control

## Module Title
Unit-07: Heat Control

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Learn how cooking actually works.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills,
safe habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- saute
- pan preheating
- oil control
- browning vs. steaming
- roasting
- oven control
- caramelization
- moist heat
- grains
- simmering
- liquid ratios

## What You Will Do
- observe heat cues
- practice hot-station setup
- compare browning and steaming
- cook grains or moist-heat items

## What You Will Turn In
- heat cue exit ticket
- lab reflection
- hot-station reset check

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- heat control handout
- heat control lab handout
- slide deck content
