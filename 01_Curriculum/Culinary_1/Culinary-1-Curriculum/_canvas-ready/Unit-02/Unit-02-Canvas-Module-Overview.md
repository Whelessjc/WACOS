# Unit-02 Canvas Module Overview: Food Handler and Stewardship

## Module Title
Unit-02: Food Handler and Stewardship

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Safety becomes behavior.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills, safe
habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- personal hygiene
- cleaning and sanitation
- cross contamination
- allergens
- time and temperature
- dish systems
- thermometer use
- stewardship jobs

## What You Will Do
- practice stewardship jobs
- use dish systems
- connect Food Handler ideas to brownie and chicken temperature labs

## What You Will Turn In
- stewardship checklist
- food safety reflection
- temperature or lab check

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- stewardship jobs handout
- food handler lab handout
- slide deck content
