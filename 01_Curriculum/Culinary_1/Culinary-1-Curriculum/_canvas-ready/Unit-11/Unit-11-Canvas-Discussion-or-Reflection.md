# Unit-11 Canvas Discussion or Reflection: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Prompt
What evidence shows you are ready for more independent kitchen work?

## Student Directions
Write one clear paragraph or answer the questions in a numbered list. Use examples from class, lab, service, cleanup,
or team work.

## Guiding Questions
- What did you practice in this unit?
- What safety or cleanup habit mattered most?
- How did your work help your group or station?
- Where did you need correction or more practice?
- What evidence shows you can work with more independence?
- How does this connect to Bistro, service, or readiness?

## Optional Sentence Starters
- One thing I practiced was...
- A safety or cleanup habit I used was...
- My group worked better when...
- I still need to practice...
- This connects to Bistro because...

## Teacher Note
Look for specific classroom evidence. The best responses name what the student did, what they noticed, and what they
should repeat or improve next time.
