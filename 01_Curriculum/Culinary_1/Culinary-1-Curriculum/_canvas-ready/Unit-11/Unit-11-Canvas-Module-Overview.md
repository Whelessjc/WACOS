# Unit-11 Canvas Module Overview: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Module Title
Unit-11: Bridge to Culinary 2

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Independence and readiness.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills, safe
habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- independent prep lists
- limited service ownership
- skill inventory
- self-assessment
- reflection
- Culinary 2 readiness

## What You Will Do
- complete a skill inventory
- build a prep list
- own a limited service role
- reflect with evidence

## What You Will Turn In
- skill inventory
- readiness reflection
- final reset check

## Materials Needed

- skill inventory

- slide deck content
