# Unit-09 Canvas Module Overview: Flavor Systems

## Module Title
Unit-09: Flavor Systems

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Move beyond recipes and learn how flavor is built.** This unit is about real
cooking, real skills, safe habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- salt
- acid
- fat
- aromatics
- herbs
- spices
- tasting
- adjustment
- flavor families
- cultural respect

## What You Will Do
- taste safely
- make small adjustments
- compare flavor families
- use respectful language
- reset allergen/tasting areas

## What You Will Turn In
- flavor observation
- adjustment reflection
- tasting/reset check

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- flavor systems handout
- flavor lab handout
- slide deck content
