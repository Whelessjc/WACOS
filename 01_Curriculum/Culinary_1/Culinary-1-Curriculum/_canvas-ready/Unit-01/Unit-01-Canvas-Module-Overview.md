# Unit-01 Canvas Module Overview: Orientation and Systems

## Module Title
Unit-01: Orientation and Systems

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Learn the room before cooking.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills,
safe habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- entry routine
- uniform readiness
- huddle
- handwashing
- measuring stations
- station setup
- cleanup/reset
- pancake lab

## What You Will Do
- practice the daily routine
- find important kitchen areas
- measure at stations
- complete the pancake lab and reset

## What You Will Turn In
- student packet check
- checklist
- pancake lab reflection

## Materials Needed
- Unit-01 student packet
- Unit-01 checklist
- pancake lab handout
- slide deck content
