# Unit-10 Canvas Module Overview: Protein as Application

## Module Title
Unit-10: Protein as Application

## Student-Facing Overview
In this module, you will work on: **Protein tests fundamentals.** This unit is about real cooking, real skills, safe
habits, teamwork, stewardship, and leaving the kitchen ready for the next group.

## What You Will Learn
- chicken
- ground turkey
- eggs
- searing
- doneness
- resting
- seasoning
- slicing
- thermometer use
- food safety

## What You Will Do
- set raw/clean zones
- use thermometers
- practice searing and resting
- apply knife, heat, mise en place, and flavor skills

## What You Will Turn In
- protein safety check
- temperature evidence
- lab reflection

## Materials Needed
- student packet
- protein cookery handout
- protein lab handout
- slide deck content
