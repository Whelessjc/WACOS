# Google Upload and Conversion Guide

## Uploading to Google Drive
1. Open Google Drive.
2. Create a folder named `Culinary Arts Management 1`.
3. Upload the files from `FINAL-DELIVERY`.
4. Keep the folder structure if possible.

## Opening DOCX as Google Docs
1. Upload the `.docx` file to Google Drive.
2. Right-click the file.
3. Choose `Open with` > `Google Docs`.
4. Review headings, page breaks, checkboxes, and tables.
5. Rename the Google Doc if needed.

## Opening PPTX as Google Slides
1. Upload the `.pptx` file to Google Drive.
2. Right-click the file.
3. Choose `Open with` > `Google Slides`.
4. Review slide spacing, images, and speaker notes.

## Exporting PDFs for the Printer
1. Open the Google Doc.
2. Choose `File` > `Download` > `PDF Document`.
3. Check page breaks before sending to print.
