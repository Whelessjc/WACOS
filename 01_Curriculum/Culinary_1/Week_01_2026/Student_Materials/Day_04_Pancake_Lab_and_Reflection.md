# First Lab: Pancakes

**Culinary 1 - Day 4**

> Drafted for instructor review before classroom use.

## Purpose

Read first. Prepare before acting. Cook with awareness. Present the work professionally. Reset for the next group.

## Before Heat

- [ ] Kitchen Mode complete
- [ ] Hands washed
- [ ] Recipe read completely
- [ ] Team knows the first three actions
- [ ] Ingredients measured
- [ ] Equipment and mise en place ready
- [ ] Towel, dish, and trash plan clear
- [ ] Instructor approval received

## While Cooking

- Watch for bubbles and set edges.
- Observe color before flipping.
- Adjust only as directed.
- Keep towels away from heat.
- Communicate clearly near hot equipment.
- Place finished pancakes in the instructor-designated low oven.

## Presentation and Eating

- Plate the group's pancakes professionally.
- Display the plate as directed.
- Do not eat until every group has finished and the instructor releases the class.
- Follow instructor-confirmed directions for syrup, toppings, leftovers, containers, and food leaving the room.

## Reset

- [ ] Heat off as directed
- [ ] Dishes handled through the correct system
- [ ] Counters cleaned and sanitized
- [ ] Tools returned
- [ ] Towels routed correctly
- [ ] Trash checked
- [ ] Floor checked
- [ ] Station ready for the next group
