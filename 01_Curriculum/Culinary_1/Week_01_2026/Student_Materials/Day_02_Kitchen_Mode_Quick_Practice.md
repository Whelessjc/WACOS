# Kitchen Mode Quick Practice

**Culinary 1 - Day 2**

> Drafted for instructor review before classroom use.

Kitchen Mode means you are ready, aware, and purposeful.

## Entry

Put these actions in the instructor's correct order:

- ___ Go to the assigned starting place.
- ___ Place belongings where they belong.
- ___ Check the agenda and readiness expectations.
- ___ Wash hands when directed.

## Speak So Others Can Work Safely

Write the best word or phrase: **behind, hot, sharp, corner, excuse me**

1. You are walking behind a classmate: ____________________
2. You are approaching a blind corner: ____________________
3. You need space to pass without startling someone: ____________________
4. You are near an approved hot item: ____________________
5. You are carrying an approved sharp item as directed: ____________________

## Reset

Circle the station problem that must be fixed:

- Tools returned / tools left out
- Counter sanitized / crumbs left behind
- Towel routed correctly / towel beside heat
- Floor checked / spill ignored
- Dishes moving through the correct system / dishes abandoned

Complete: My station is ready for the next class when __________________________.
