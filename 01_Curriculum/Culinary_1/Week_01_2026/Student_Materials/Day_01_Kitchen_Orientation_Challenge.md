# Kitchen Orientation Challenge

**Culinary 1 - Day 1**

> Drafted for instructor review before classroom use.

Work with your group. Walk, observe, and communicate. Leave every item and area ready for the next group. Point to fixed or safety equipment; do not operate it.

## Make Three Connections

Choose three items or areas. Tell your group:

1. Why does it have a specific location?
2. What could go wrong if it is missing or returned incorrectly?
3. How does knowing the location help the next person?
