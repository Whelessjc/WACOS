# Culinary 1 Week 1 Slide Source

> Concise classroom slide content. Drafted for instructor review before classroom use.

## Day 1 - Welcome and Kitchen Orientation

### Slide 1 - Welcome to West Ashley Culinary

- Classroom to Table
- High School, High Standards
- Today: learn the room and begin Kitchen Mode

### Slide 2 - Bellringer

- One meal you already know how to prepare
- One meal you would like to learn

### Slide 3 - Learning Target

I can locate important kitchen tools and areas and explain why organization matters.

### Slide 4 - Kitchen Orientation Challenge

- Work with your group.
- Walk. Observe. Communicate.
- Leave every item where it belongs.
- Point to safety and fixed equipment. Do not operate it.

### Slide 5 - Closure

Name one place you can now locate and one way organization protects the team.

## Day 2 - Kitchen Mode and Basic Routines

### Slide 6 - Bellringer

From memory, list the first three actions when class begins.

### Slide 7 - Learning Target

I can enter Kitchen Mode and practice the routines that protect people and the room.

### Slide 8 - Kitchen Mode

- Belongings placed correctly
- Agenda and readiness checked
- Hands washed when directed
- Starting place reached
- Ready, aware, purposeful

### Slide 9 - Speak So Others Can Work Safely

Behind. Hot. Sharp. Corner. Excuse me.

### Slide 10 - Reset Means Ready

Tools. Counters. Dishes. Towels. Trash. Floor.

### Slide 11 - Closure

Kitchen Mode begins when I ___, and it protects the team by ___.

## Day 3 - Read Before You Cook

### Slide 12 - Bellringer

Scan the pancake recipe. Circle the first action verb.

### Slide 13 - Learning Target

I can read the recipe, identify what we need, and prepare mise en place.

### Slide 14 - Read First

Find:

- Ingredients
- Equipment
- Measurements
- Action verbs
- Heat cues

### Slide 15 - Mise en Place

Tools, ingredients, and the plan ready before cooking.

### Slide 16 - Friday Ready Check

- Recipe read
- Measuring tools selected
- First three actions known
- Heat stays off until instructor approval

## Day 4 - First Lab: Pancakes

### Slide 17 - Today's Standard

Read first. Cook second.

### Slide 18 - Learning Target

I can prepare mise en place, cook with approval, present our work, and reset the station.

### Slide 19 - Before Heat

- Hands washed
- Recipe read
- Mise en place complete
- Instructor approval received

### Slide 20 - Watch the Pancake

Bubbles. Set edges. Color. Heat response.

### Slide 21 - Hold and Present

- Finished pancakes go to the designated low oven.
- Plate the group's work professionally.
- Display before eating.
- Wait until all groups finish and the instructor releases the class.

### Slide 22 - Reset

The lab is not finished when the food is finished.

### Slide 23 - Reflection

- What system helped your group?
- What heat cue did you notice?
- What will you improve next time?
