# Unit 7: Baking and Pastry Foundations

Approximate timing: Semester 2

## Purpose

Baking and pastry reinforce precision, patience, sequencing, and temperature discipline. The goal is not a separate pastry course. The goal is to strengthen professional habits through formulas and timing-sensitive work.

## Primary Sources

- `../../03_Semester_2_Module_Map.md`
- `../../01_Modular_Pacing_Guide.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Weigh accurately in grams.
- Follow formulas without improvising too early.
- Practice mixing methods.
- Understand fermentation and proofing.
- Control custards and temperature-sensitive items.
- Evaluate texture and doneness.

## Suggested Recipe Vehicles

- muffins
- focaccia
- baguette-style bread
- custards
- ice cream bases
- simple plated dessert components

## Evidence

Students should follow formulas, track timing, and describe how process choices affected final texture.

## Build-Out Notes

Create scaling checks, formula reading handouts, baking production sheets, and product evaluation rubrics.
