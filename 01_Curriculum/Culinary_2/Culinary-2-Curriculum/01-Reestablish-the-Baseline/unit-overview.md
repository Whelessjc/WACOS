# Unit 1: Reestablish the Baseline

Approximate timing: Weeks 1-3

## Purpose

Students return to the kitchen and rebuild the habits that make advanced work possible: uniform readiness, station setup, sanitation, heat control, seasoning, timing, mise en place, and complete reset.

This is not review for its own sake. It is recalibration.

## Primary Sources

- `../../00_Master_Framework.md`
- `../../01_Modular_Pacing_Guide.md`
- `../../02_Semester_1_Weekly_Guide.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Start class prepared and in uniform.
- Set up a station without drift.
- Read and follow the posted agenda.
- Practice clean-as-you-go until it becomes visible.
- Use heat intentionally.
- Taste, season, and adjust.
- Finish with a reset kitchen.

## Suggested Recipe Vehicles

- sauteed vegetables
- rice or Carolina Gold rice
- red rice prep
- Hoppin' John prep
- vinaigrettes
- simple emulsified dressings
- soup or sauce base prep

## Evidence

Students should be able to explain what they are doing now, what comes next, what success looks like, when heat stops, and what must be cleaned before moving on.

## Build-Out Notes

Create a student packet, station setup checklist, baseline practical check, and short line check cards for heat, seasoning, and reset routines.
