# Unit 8: Service Repetition and Application

Approximate timing: Semester 2

## Purpose

Students move from participating in Bistro to helping carry the plan. Service repetition is where habits become visible.

## Primary Sources

- `../../03_Semester_2_Module_Map.md`
- `../../04_Bistro_Integration.md`
- `../../../02_Bistro/README.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Call plans and timing with more independence.
- Support teammates under pressure.
- Maintain station standards.
- Scale production thoughtfully.
- Communicate problems early.
- Recover from service issues calmly.

## Suggested Applications

- station lead rotation
- prep list ownership
- service role rotation
- expo support
- production scaling task
- service debrief

## Evidence

Students should show increasing ability to anticipate needs, protect quality, and communicate clearly during prep and service.

## Build-Out Notes

Create station leadership rubrics, service role cards, production scaling tasks, and weekly Bistro evidence logs.
