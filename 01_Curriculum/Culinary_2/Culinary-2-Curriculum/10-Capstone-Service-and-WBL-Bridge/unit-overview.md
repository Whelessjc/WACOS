# Unit 10: Capstone, Service, and WBL Bridge

Approximate timing: Final weeks

## Purpose

The final stretch should ask students to execute with composure, explain decisions, and show readiness for further culinary study, Bistro responsibility, or work-based learning.

## Primary Sources

- `../../03_Semester_2_Module_Map.md`
- `../../05_CKC_Alignment.md`
- `../../10_West_Ashley_25_Technique_Map.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Execute a meaningful practical task or service role.
- Plan prep and timing.
- Communicate under pressure.
- Maintain sanitation and reset standards.
- Reflect on performance using evidence.
- Connect kitchen behavior to workplace readiness.

## Possible Capstone Formats

- Bistro integration role
- composed plate
- tasting menu component
- service leadership role
- production item scaled for service
- CKC readiness demonstration

## Evidence

Students should be able to answer:

- What was the plan?
- What changed?
- What did you adjust?
- How did you communicate?
- What would you do next time?

## Build-Out Notes

Create capstone options, student planning sheets, practical scoring rubrics, and WBL readiness reflection forms.
