# Unit 5: Sauces, Moist Heat, and Rice Dishes

Approximate timing: Weeks 10-14

## Purpose

Students learn to control structure, liquid, texture, simmering, and seasoning. Sauces and rice dishes make mistakes visible, which makes them valuable teaching tools.

## Primary Sources

- `../../01_Modular_Pacing_Guide.md`
- `../../02_Semester_1_Weekly_Guide.md`
- `../../10_West_Ashley_25_Technique_Map.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Control roux color and texture.
- Reduce liquids with purpose.
- Build and repair emulsions.
- Simmer without scorching.
- Season in layers.
- Understand rice dishes as technique vehicles.

## Suggested Recipe Vehicles

- macaroni and cheese
- pan sauce
- shrimp Alfredo
- soups
- braised dishes
- chicken bog or chicken perloo
- red rice
- crab rice
- Hoppin' John

## Evidence

Students should be able to name what happened when a sauce breaks, a roux scorches, rice overcooks, or soup lacks structure.

## Build-Out Notes

Create sauce problem cards, simmer observation checks, rice dish comparison notes, and short correction reflections.
