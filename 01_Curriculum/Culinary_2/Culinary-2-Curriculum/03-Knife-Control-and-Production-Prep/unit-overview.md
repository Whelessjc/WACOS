# Unit 3: Knife Control and Production Prep

Approximate timing: Weeks 1-6, then recurring

## Purpose

Knife work in Culinary 2 is production work. Students are expected to produce usable prep with better consistency, speed, sanitation, and awareness of yield.

## Primary Sources

- `../../00_Master_Framework.md`
- `../../01_Modular_Pacing_Guide.md`
- `../../10_West_Ashley_25_Technique_Map.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Use safe grip, stable board setup, and controlled movement.
- Produce consistent cuts for actual recipes.
- Label, store, and hand off prep correctly.
- Improve speed without losing accuracy.
- Understand that prep quality affects service quality.

## Suggested Recipe Vehicles

- mirepoix, trinity, sofrito, or curry base
- red rice prep
- soup base
- salsa or chow chow
- salad components
- Bistro prep lists

## Evidence

Students should produce prep that another station can use without rework.

## Build-Out Notes

Create cut-standard visuals, timed prep checks, yield notes, and a production prep rubric.
