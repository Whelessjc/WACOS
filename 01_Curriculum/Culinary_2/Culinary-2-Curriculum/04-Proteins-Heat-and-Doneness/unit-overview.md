# Unit 4: Proteins, Heat, and Doneness

Approximate timing: Weeks 6-10

## Purpose

Students build confidence with proteins by practicing safe handling, browning, carryover, thermometer use, and doneness judgment. If students cannot cook proteins cleanly, later work is fragile.

## Primary Sources

- `../../01_Modular_Pacing_Guide.md`
- `../../02_Semester_1_Weekly_Guide.md`
- `../../05_CKC_Alignment.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Handle proteins safely.
- Choose and control heat.
- Use thermometers correctly.
- Recognize carryover cooking.
- Build simple pan sauces.
- Recover from common mistakes without panic.

## Suggested Recipe Vehicles

- roast chicken
- pan-seared chicken breast
- stewed chicken
- steak with pan sauce
- fish cookery
- shrimp cookery
- fried fish
- shrimp and grits

## Evidence

Students should explain doneness using temperature, visual cues, timing, and carryover rather than guessing.

## Build-Out Notes

Create protein handling checklists, thermometer checks, doneness line checks, and practical rubrics aligned to CKC evidence.
