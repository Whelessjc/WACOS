# Unit 2: Bistro Systems and Soft Launch

Approximate timing: Weeks 4-5

## Purpose

Students learn how Bistro work functions as the central instructional model. The goal is not to simulate a restaurant for novelty. The goal is to teach responsibility, communication, timing, follow-through, and service awareness.

## Primary Sources

- `../../04_Bistro_Integration.md`
- `../../02_Semester_1_Weekly_Guide.md`
- `../../../02_Bistro/README.md`

## Learning Priorities

- Understand Thursday prep and Friday service rhythm.
- Practice ticket flow and station communication.
- Use prep lists and labels accurately.
- Move with urgency without becoming frantic.
- Reset stations for the next crew.
- Treat guests and classmates with hospitality.

## Suggested Applications

- mock service
- station setup drill
- ticket communication practice
- Bistro prep labeling check
- limited or soft-launch Friday service
- station handoff reflection

## Evidence

Students should show that they can receive a task, communicate status, finish prep cleanly, and understand how their station affects service.

## Build-Out Notes

Create station cards, mock ticket sets, prep list templates, service debrief forms, and a soft-launch rubric.
