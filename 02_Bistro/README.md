# 02_Bistro

This folder contains current Bistro operating materials.

## Belongs Here

- Bistro operating guides.
- Menus.
- Prep lists.
- Production plans.
- Service plans.
- Station guides.
- Bistro role documents.
- Bistro SOPs.
- Guest flow and service recovery tools.

## Does Not Belong Here

- General curriculum maps.
- General grading policy.
- Raw recipe archives.
- Equipment safety manuals not specific to Bistro service.
- Historical Bistro drafts unless clearly archived.

## Relationship to the Core Manual

The Bistro is the central instructional model for West Ashley Culinary. Bistro documents must protect safety, learning, hospitality, service flow, and student responsibility.

## Caution

POS, payment, reservation, customer, and cash-handling procedures require current instructor/admin confirmation before being treated as official.

## Current SOP Set

Use `SOPs/` for the current Life of an Order, service standards, and Dining, Kitchen, Support, and Barista team procedures. The four current team names are Dining, Kitchen, Support, and Barista.

## 2026-2027 Launch Calendar

Week 4 (August 31-September 4) begins Bistro systems, mock service, and optional tightly controlled soft-opening work. Because September 4 is an early-release Friday, the first normal public service is Friday, September 11 in Week 5, subject to readiness and safety. See `../00_Core/2026-07-20_Calendar_Week_Mapping.md`.
