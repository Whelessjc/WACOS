# Kitchen Team SOP (Cooks)

Current as of 2026-07. Source: real Cooks Side Work Checklist in current use.

## Opening Equipment Setup

### General

- Soap and sanitizer stocked below the station.
- Gloves stocked.
- Ovens and griddle turned on.
- Tasting spoons out.
- Aluminum foil sheets and half parchment sheets stocked.
- Kitchen spoons in a small bain marie.
- Dry side towels folded (2 per person).
- Menu on hand.
- Large wooden cutting board with knife.
- Trash can on station.
- Timer.
- Speed racks with 6 full sheet pans.
- Tongs in a medium bain marie (by the convection oven).
- Griddle scrapers x2.
- Salt and pepper shakers x2.
- Tongs x2.
- Thermometer.
- Butter roller.
- Silver bullets.

### Breakfast-Specific

- 1 oz ladle x2, 4 oz ladle.
- Large non-stick pan (red handle).
- Large high-heat silicone spatula.
- Griddle steam lid x2.
- Griddle spatulas x2 (blue and red only).
- Hotel pan with rack (for bacon).
- 1/3 pan with 1-cup measuring scoop (for burrito production).
- Water bottle.

### Lunch-Specific

- Hot-holding countertop food warmer with 2 deep half pans.
- 2 small bain maries with portion scoops.
- Pizza peel x2, pizza rack, pizza roller cutter.

## Production by Menu Item

### Brunch Prep (7:30-10 service window)

- **Belgian Waffles**: batter, clarified butter, powdered sugar, syrup, berries (blueberry, strawberry, blackberry).
- **Bodega**: hamburger buns, clarified butter, eggs blended with milk, bacon, American cheese, salt and pepper mix.
- **Breakfast Burrito**: tortillas, sausage, eggs, cheddar, burrito sriracha sauce.
- Cook bacon (quarter sheet pan with rack). Prepare pancake batter; prep side items (blueberries, chocolate chips) in a 1/9 pan with lid.

### Lunch Prep (10-2:20 service window)

- **Flaky Pan Pizza**: pizza dough, pizza sauce, mozzarella, parmesan, basil garlic oil.
- **Smoked Chicken Mac and Cheese**: chicken, mac and cheese, scallions.
- **Shrimp Salad Roll**: shrimp salad, rolls.

Mise en place for all items comes from the station's stocked lowboys - see the current prep list for exact par levels.

## Closing Duties

- Griddle off, area cleaned on all sides; griddle pan emptied.
- Grill off and clean.
- Saute range off, clean, and free of food.
- Line convection oven off (or in cool-down) and cleaned.
- Line fryer off, strained, and cleaned.
- Lowboys clean; items wrapped and labeled.
- Heat lamps off; shelf clean and sanitary.
- All food items stored.
- Small trash bags emptied into the large can.
