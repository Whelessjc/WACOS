# Barista Team SOP

Current as of 2026-07. Source: real Barista Station Checklist and Barista Station Recipe Sheet in current use.

## Opening Checklist

- Espresso machine turned on and preheated.
- Grinder calibrated (adjust grind size if needed).
- Knock box emptied and cleaned.
- Steam wands purged and wiped down.
- Milk pitchers cleaned and chilled.
- Coffee beans stocked (regular, decaf, specialty).
- Syrups and flavorings stocked (vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, etc.).
- Tea station stocked (black, green, herbal, decaf).
- Sugar, sweeteners, honey, and stir sticks restocked.
- Milk options available (whole, skim, almond, oat, soy).
- Ice bin filled with fresh ice.
- Cups, lids, sleeves, and straws stocked (hot and cold).
- Blenders and shakers cleaned and ready.
- Menu displayed (printed, digital, or felt board updated).
- Pastry cabinet stocked with fresh baked goods; items labeled and arranged.
- Deli paper, tongs, and spatulas stocked for serving pastries.
- Bakery trash can emptied and lined; general trash cans emptied and relined.

## During Service

- Maintain a clean, organized workstation; wipe counters and equipment regularly.
- Purge the steam wand after each use.
- Check espresso shot timing for consistency (18-22 seconds per shot).
- Keep milk properly refrigerated; discard expired milk.
- Monitor syrup and milk levels, restocking as needed.
- Communicate with servers and kitchen staff for special orders.
- Follow standard drink recipes for consistency (see below).
- Restock and rotate pastries; coordinate with servers on pastry orders; remove stale or expired items promptly.

## Closing Checklist

- Turn off the espresso machine and let it cool.
- Empty and clean the knock box.
- Remove, clean, and dry milk pitchers.
- Wash, sanitize, and air-dry all utensils and tools.
- Clean and purge the steam wand thoroughly.
- Wipe down the grinder and clean the grounds tray.
- Discard old brewed coffee and tea.
- Restock cups, lids, sleeves, and straws for the next shift.
- Wrap and label leftover bakery items for the next day; discard stale/unsellable pastries; turn off bakery cabinet lights.
- Sweep and mop the barista station area.
- Take out trash and recycling.

## Drink Recipe Reference

### Drip Coffee
Fill the grinder with beans, grind between Extra Fine and Fine, timer setting #12. Pour ground coffee into a filter-lined basket, insert into the machine. Fill the carafe with water, lift the black flap and pour water in. Confirm basket and carafe are in place, press START. Serve black or with cream, half-and-half, milk, and/or sugar.

### Americano (Hot)
2 oz espresso (double shot) + 6-8 oz hot water. Pull the shots, then add hot water.

### Espresso
Double (2 oz) = 14 g coffee. Push the left button, programmed for a 2 oz pull.

### Cappuccino
Ratio 1:1:1 - 2 oz espresso, 2 oz steamed milk, 2 oz thick milk foam.

*(Additional drink builds - lattes, iced coffee, matcha, specialty drinks - follow the same station recipe sheet; confirm current ratios with the posted recipe card before service.)*
