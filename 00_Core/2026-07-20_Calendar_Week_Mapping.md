# Calendar Week Mapping - 2026-2027

Date adopted: July 20, 2026

## Purpose

Map the pacing-guide week labels used across Culinary 1 and Culinary 2 to the CCSD 2026-2027 Academic Calendar, updated May 19, 2026. This note governs dated planning for the 2026-2027 school year only.

## Mapping Method

Week 1 is the Monday-Friday block containing the first student day: August 10-14, 2026. Students begin Tuesday, August 11.

- A Monday-Friday block with at least one instructional day retains a week number, even when shortened by a holiday, workday, or early release.
- A Monday-Friday block with no instructional days is skipped rather than assigned an instructional week number.
- Fall Break, the two full Winter Break blocks, and Spring Break are therefore not numbered instructional weeks.
- Week labels remain planning anchors, not promises of five full instructional days.

## Post-Guide Calendar Buffer

The 36-week curriculum spine ends May 14 under this mapping. Two additional calendar blocks remain:

- May 17-21, 2027
- May 24-28, 2027, with early release and the last student day on Thursday, May 27; Friday, May 28 is a teacher workday

Use this period for testing interruptions, make-up work, portfolio completion, inventory, deep cleaning, equipment shutdown, graduation-related disruptions, and other year-end requirements. Do not add a new required content unit here until the detailed testing and graduation calendars are known.

## Bistro Launch Decision

Week 4 remains the confirmed Bistro launch window because training, mock service, and a controlled soft opening begin then. Because September 4 is an early-release Friday, the first normal public Bistro service is Friday, September 11 in Week 5. The instructor retains authority to reduce, postpone, or pause service for readiness or safety.

## Remaining Calendar Dependencies

- Exact Hospitality Scholars and Explore Charleston visit dates.
- The guest-chef roster and partner availability for the tasting menu.
- Detailed AP, EOC, exam, graduation, and school-event calendars.
- Weather make-up use of November 23-24 or April 26.
- Culinary 1 spring-cohort readiness; Week 23 remains the relaunch fallback.
