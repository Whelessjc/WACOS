# Equipment, Facility, and Safety

## Purpose
Document safety expectations, facility routines, equipment procedures, cleaning systems, and emergency response.

## Safety Priorities
- Food Handler/ServSafe habits.
- Knife safety.
- Heat and burn prevention.
- Chemical safety.
- Dish systems.
- Allergen awareness.
- Equipment authorization.
- Storage, labeling, dating, FIFO.
- Emergency procedures.

## Foundational Systems Students Must Know
- Handwashing.
- Triple sink setup.
- Dish machine setup.
- Sanitizer test strips.
- Clean vs sanitize.
- Towel and laundry flow.
- Trash and recycling.
- Floor care.
- Cooler/freezer organization.
- Smallwares stocking.
- Knife carry, storage, and cutting board stability.

## Equipment Authorization
Students may use equipment only after instruction, demonstration, and instructor approval. Culinary 2 students may have more independence, but the standard is higher, not looser.

## Cleaning Standard
Clean-as-you-go is not a slogan. Students should be able to notice and fix:
- Empty sanitizer.
- Full trash.
- Missing labels.
- Dirty handles.
- Wet floors.
- Unstocked tools.
- Unreturned equipment.
- Food stored uncovered or undated.
