# 08_Equipment_and_Safety

This folder contains equipment, facility, safety, cleaning, emergency, and sanitation materials.

## Belongs Here

- Equipment authorization documents.
- Facility maps.
- Shutoff locations.
- Emergency procedures.
- Cleaning SOPs.
- Sanitation SOPs.
- Chemical safety.
- Knife, heat, fire, and equipment safety guidance.

## Does Not Belong Here

- General curriculum documents.
- Recipes unless safety controls are the main purpose.
- AI-generated safety instructions without review.
- Historical SOPs unless clearly archived.

## Relationship to the Core Manual

Safety governs all work. No production goal, Bistro service, or lesson plan overrides safety and sanitation requirements.

## Caution

Safety-sensitive documents require instructor review and, when applicable, school or district confirmation.
