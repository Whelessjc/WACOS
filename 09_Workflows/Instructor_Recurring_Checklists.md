# Instructor Recurring Checklists

## Use

Print or copy the section needed. These checks support instructor operations; they do not replace current curriculum, Bistro SOPs, safety requirements, district procedures, or bookkeeper controls.

## Daily

### Before Students

- [ ] Coffee ready.
- [ ] Groceries received and stored, if applicable.
- [ ] Email, calendar, Canvas, and current planning systems reviewed.
- [ ] Kitchen walkthrough complete: safety, cleanliness, storage, and equipment.
- [ ] Agenda, learning targets, lesson, resources, and shutdown time ready.
- [ ] Instructor mise en place complete.

### Class Startup

- [ ] Greet students.
- [ ] Attendance, ID, uniform or dress code, phones, and headphones checked.
- [ ] Announcements and daily check-in complete.
- [ ] Agenda, learning targets, prep list, and cleaning duties reviewed.
- [ ] Stations assigned and set.
- [ ] Hands washed; mise en place and chef check complete.

### End of Day

- [ ] Safety and cleanliness walkthrough complete.
- [ ] Food stored and labeled; equipment secured; room reset.
- [ ] Lab or live-assessment evidence recorded.
- [ ] Grades updated when scheduled.
- [ ] Daily journal or reflection completed.
- [ ] Email reviewed.
- [ ] Next instructional day ready.

## Weekly

- [ ] Lesson plans checked against pacing and calendar.
- [ ] Weekly planning document updated.
- [ ] Lab and Employability sheets printed or prepared.
- [ ] Food and coffee inventory completed; orders placed.
- [ ] Previous Bistro manager's log reviewed.
- [ ] Prior Bistro deposit and required external paperwork completed.
- [ ] Monday-Wednesday instruction protected and grading backlog cleared.
- [ ] Thursday prep lists, recipes, station assignments, roles, equipment, and storage ready.
- [ ] Friday reservations or queue materials, Square, service stations, and guest flow ready.
- [ ] Bistro close, register close, receipts, bookkeeper paperwork, deposit, and manager's log completed.
- [ ] Weekly reflection captured: wins, problems, safety, inventory, grading, and reteach.
- [ ] Next week's learning, labs, resources, and risks identified.

## Quarterly

- [ ] Complete one documented deep clean using current safety and sanitation guidance.
- [ ] Inspect all stations, hoods, coolers, storage, dish systems, and hard-to-reach areas.
- [ ] Record maintenance, repair, replacement, pest, or facility concerns.
- [ ] Review recurring inventory and ordering problems.
- [ ] Confirm frequently used forms, checklists, and external procedures are current.
- [ ] Review WACOS workflow notes for durable changes that need the Update Workflow.

## Semester

- [ ] Review pacing completed, delayed, compressed, and omitted work.
- [ ] Review Bistro launch, service rhythm, student readiness, and recurring operational problems.
- [ ] Review grading workflow and unresolved evidence backlogs without changing grading policy.
- [ ] Review inventory, equipment, maintenance, and replacement priorities.
- [ ] Complete end-of-semester or year-end storage, shutdown, and reset requirements.
- [ ] Review the Core Manual, Decision Register, TODO, and current calendar dependencies.
- [ ] Archive or retire obsolete working materials where appropriate.
- [ ] Submit durable program changes through the WACOS Update Workflow.
