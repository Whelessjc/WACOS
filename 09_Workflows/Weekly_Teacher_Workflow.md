# Weekly Teacher Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow to run the instructional week, protect Monday-Wednesday learning, prepare the Bistro on Thursday, and execute Bistro Service on Friday. Adjust timing for the current [calendar mapping](../00_Core/2026-07-20_Calendar_Week_Mapping.md), interruptions, student readiness, and safety.

## Every Morning

- Make coffee.
- Walk the kitchen for cleanliness, safety, storage, and overnight issues.
- Put away received groceries when needed.
- Open the computer and review email and the day's external systems.
- Review the instructor checklist and calendar.
- Prepare the lesson, agenda, learning targets, resources, and demonstration.
- Complete instructor mise en place before students arrive.

## Every Class Startup

1. Greet students.
2. Check attendance, ID, dress code or uniform, phones, and headphones.
3. Share announcements, opportunities, competitions, and jobs.
4. Review the daily agenda and learning targets.
5. Complete the daily check-in.
6. Review the prep list and cleaning duty.
7. Assign or confirm stations.
8. Wash hands and complete mise en place.
9. Conduct the chef check.
10. Begin work.

Post the shutdown time. Unless the schedule requires an earlier close, stop cooking 15 minutes before the end of class and begin the [closing standard](../00_Core/08_Daily_and_Weekly_Systems.md#closing-standard).

## Monday - Launch the Week

### Before Students

- Review lesson plans against current pacing and student progress.
- Verify the school calendar and known interruptions.
- Update the weekly planning Google Doc or current planning document.
- Print weekly lab sheets and Employability sheets.
- Post the agenda, objective, and shutdown time.
- Prepare the lesson display and classroom resources.
- Open the Bistro ordering form when service is active.
- Post current volunteer or program opportunities.

### Instruction

- Introduce new learning.
- Demonstrate and provide student practice.
- Use live checks and begin grading during reset when practical.
- Protect the shutdown and room reset.

### Planning and Administration

- Complete food inventory.
- Place the food order.
- Place the coffee order.
- Review the previous Bistro manager's log and identify one or two feedback points.
- If Friday's Bistro deposit was deferred, complete the secured deposit and required external records Monday.
- Prepare Tuesday completely.
- Finish the day's lab grading.

## Tuesday - Teach, Practice, and Grade

- Teach new content.
- Provide focused practice and feedback.
- Complete live or formative checks.
- Grade lab work during reset when practical.
- Finish remaining grading and update grades in the district system.
- Prepare Wednesday completely.

## Wednesday - Finish Instruction and Plan Ahead

- Complete the week's planned new instruction.
- Use practice and formative checks to identify readiness and reteach needs.
- Grade all remaining work and clear the lab backlog.
- Build next week's lessons against pacing and calendar constraints.
- Identify next week's labs.
- Gather or identify recipes, resources, equipment, and materials.
- Confirm what students must know before Thursday prep and Friday service.

## Thursday - Bistro Prep and Alignment

### Before Students

- Print prep lists.
- Print station recipes.
- Print or post station assignments and Friday roles.
- Post the prep agenda and shutdown time.
- Stage equipment.
- Clear and label storage.

### During Class

- Execute Bistro Prep.
- Reinforce Friday roles, standards, and escalation paths.
- Monitor food safety, production pace, labels, storage, and station readiness.
- Stop production at the posted shutdown time.
- Complete the hard close: stations reset, sinks and dish systems closed correctly, scrap traps emptied, screens replaced, floors cleaned, coolers organized and labeled, and trash and recycling removed.

### Readiness Check

- Update the weekly planning document.
- Identify product, staffing, equipment, timing, and safety risks.
- Verify all prep is complete or assign a controlled recovery plan.
- Leave the room turnkey for Friday.

Use current [Bistro Operations](../02_Bistro/Operations/05_Bistro_Operations.md) and team SOPs for service-specific requirements.

## Friday - Bistro Service

### Before Students and Service

- Walk the room.
- Verify sinks, sanitizer, stations, equipment, and coolers.
- Confirm labels, holding, recipes, and service supplies.
- Print reservations or queue sheets as currently required.
- Open Square and other approved external service systems.
- Confirm cash and check payment types and the `PBIS Incentive` and `Bistro Coin` comp reasons are available.
- Confirm the backup receipt system is ready.
- Brief roles, menu, guest flow, standards, and escalation paths.

### During Service

- Run service.
- Coach and observe.
- Capture live assessment evidence.
- Maintain food safety, quality, communication, and hospitality standards.
- Handle guest recovery using the current HEARD method.

### Close

- Complete the full kitchen, dish-area, dining, support, and Barista close.
- Close all Square tickets and confirm payment and comp types.
- Count and secure cash and checks; confirm checks are payable to WAHS.
- Download the detailed Square transactions report.
- Reconcile net sales and payment-method totals to cash and checks.
- Review PBIS Incentive and Bistro Coin comps.
- Reconcile any backup receipts.
- Complete bookkeeper paperwork and attach the Square report and required supporting records.
- Complete the deposit Friday when time permits; otherwise secure it for Monday completion.
- Complete the manager's log.
- Record grading evidence before details are lost.
- Capture a short service reflection: wins, problems, safety concerns, inventory issues, and reteach needs.

Follow the [Bistro Payment and Deposit Workflow](../02_Bistro/SOPs/Bistro_Payment_and_Deposit_Workflow.md). District and bookkeeper requirements continue to govern custody, verification, delivery, and retention.

## Weekend - Reflect and Reset the Plan

- Review the week's instruction and Bistro results.
- Review pacing against the calendar.
- Review inventory and ordering concerns.
- Review curriculum fit and missing resources.
- Identify next week's learning, labs, and Bistro needs.
- Identify any durable WACOS correction or improvement.
- Use the [WACOS Update Workflow](../00_Core/WACOS_Update_Workflow.md) for permanent changes.
